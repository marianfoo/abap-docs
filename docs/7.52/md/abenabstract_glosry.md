abstract

Term in [ABAP objects](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_objects_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). An abstract [class](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") cannot be instanced. An abstract [method](javascript:call_link\('abenmethod_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") can only be implemented in [subclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensubclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of its class. A non-abstract class or method is [concrete](javascript:call_link\('abenconcrete_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
