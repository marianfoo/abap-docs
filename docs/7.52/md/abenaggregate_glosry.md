Aggregate

Grouping of data from several lines in a [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Can be determined by means of [aggregate functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenaggregate_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [SELECT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapselect.htm) statement.
