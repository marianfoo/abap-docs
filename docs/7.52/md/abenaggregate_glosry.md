Aggregate

Grouping of data from several lines in a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Can be determined by means of [aggregate functions](javascript:call_link\('abenaggregate_function_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [SELECT](javascript:call_link\('abapselect.htm'\)) statement.
