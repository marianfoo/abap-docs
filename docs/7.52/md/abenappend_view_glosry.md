Append view

Object of the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), used to extend a [database view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with further fields of its basis tables, without making any modifications.
