basic arithmetic operation

The four basic arithmetic operations addition, subtraction, multiplication and division are realized in ABAP via the [arithmetic operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarithmetic_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ([+](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\-](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\*](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm) and [/](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm)) in [arithmetic expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenarithmetic_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and via the statements [ADD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapadd.htm), [SUBTRACT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapsubtract.htm), [MULTIPLY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapmultiply.htm) and [DIVIDE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapdivide.htm).
