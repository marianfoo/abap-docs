basic arithmetic operation

The four basic arithmetic operations addition, subtraction, multiplication and division are realized in ABAP via the [arithmetic operators](javascript:call_link\('abenarithmetic_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([+](javascript:call_link\('abenarith_operators.htm'\)), [\-](javascript:call_link\('abenarith_operators.htm'\)), [\*](javascript:call_link\('abenarith_operators.htm'\)) and [/](javascript:call_link\('abenarith_operators.htm'\))) in [arithmetic expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenarithmetic_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and via the statements [ADD](javascript:call_link\('abapadd.htm'\)), [SUBTRACT](javascript:call_link\('abapsubtract.htm'\)), [MULTIPLY](javascript:call_link\('abapmultiply.htm'\)) and [DIVIDE](javascript:call_link\('abapdivide.htm'\)).
