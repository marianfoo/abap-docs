bit expression

Formulation of a binary operation in a [calculation expression](javascript:call_link\('abencalculation_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The result of a bit expression is a byte-like value. Bit expressions occur in [reader positions of specific statements](javascript:call_link\('abenoperands_expressions.htm'\)).
