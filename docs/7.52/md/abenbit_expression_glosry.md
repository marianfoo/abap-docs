bit expression

Formulation of a binary operation in a [calculation expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencalculation_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The result of a bit expression is a byte-like value. Bit expressions occur in [reader positions of specific statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperands_expressions.htm).
