Calculation Length

Property of a [bit expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenbit_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") based on the [byte-type data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenbyte_like_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") involved. The calculation length is the length in which the operands of the expression are processed.
