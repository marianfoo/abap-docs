Calculation Length

Property of a [bit expression](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") based on the [byte-type data types](javascript:call_link\('abenbyte_like_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") involved. The calculation length is the length in which the operands of the expression are processed.
