Calendar date

Date for short. Precise time (to the day, week, or month) with respect to the Gregorian calendar. Valid content of a [date field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendate_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
