CDS role

A [CDS object](javascript:call_link\('abencds_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") defined using the statement [DEFINE ROLE](javascript:call_link\('abencds_f1_define_role.htm'\)) in [DCL source code](javascript:call_link\('abendcl_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [CDS DCL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_dcl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). This object applies a [CDS access control](javascript:call_link\('abencds_access_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") based on [access conditions](javascript:call_link\('abenaccess_condition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") when [CDS](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are accessed using [Open SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenopen_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Currently, a CDS role is assigned to every [user](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The access conditions of a CDS role evaluate the classic [authorizations](javascript:call_link\('abenauthorization_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the current user.
