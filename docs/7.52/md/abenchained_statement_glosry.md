chained statement

Abbreviated diction for multiple [ABAP-statements](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with the same beginning part. The beginning part is noted once and concluded with a colon (:). Behind that follow the remaining parts separated by commas.
