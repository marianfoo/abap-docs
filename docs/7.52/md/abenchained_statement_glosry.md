chained statement

Abbreviated diction for multiple [ABAP-statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the same beginning part. The beginning part is noted once and concluded with a colon (:). Behind that follow the remaining parts separated by commas.
