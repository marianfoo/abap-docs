classic view

[View](javascript:call_link\('abenview_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of ABAP Dictionary defined in the form-based [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [tool](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_tools.htm'\)) in [ABAP Workbench](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Classic views can be [database views](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [projection views](javascript:call_link\('abenprojection_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [maintenance views](javascript:call_link\('abenmaintenance_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and [help views](javascript:call_link\('abenhelp_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
