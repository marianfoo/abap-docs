client

Organizational unit in [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). For each [logon](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlogon_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to AS ABAP, a [client ID](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclient_identifier_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") must be specified. This ID enables a logon to a specific client. The various clients in an AS ABAP have separate [user master records](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenuser_master_record_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and separate [authorizations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenauthorization_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Client-specific data is split by the [client column](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclient_column_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [application tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenapplication_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [Application programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenapplication_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") generally run in [application clients](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenapplication_client_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). There is also a [system client](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensystem_client_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for performing system-specific or administrative tasks.
