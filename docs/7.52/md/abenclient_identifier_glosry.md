Client Identifier

Three-character [client](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclient_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") identifier that is sent to an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") upon [logon](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlogon_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In [application tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenapplication_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), client-specific data is identified with the client identifier stored in the [client column](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclient_column_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
