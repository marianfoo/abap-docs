Client Identifier

Three-character [client](javascript:call_link\('abenclient_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") identifier that is sent to an [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") upon [logon](javascript:call_link\('abenlogon_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In [application tables](javascript:call_link\('abenapplication_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), client-specific data is identified with the client identifier stored in the [client column](javascript:call_link\('abenclient_column_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
