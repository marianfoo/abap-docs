Cluster

Grouping of different data in a common storage location. Either as a [data cluster](javascript:call_link\('abendata_cluster_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in various storage media for [data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or as a [table cluster](javascript:call_link\('abentable_cluster_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([cluster tables](javascript:call_link\('abencluster_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")).
