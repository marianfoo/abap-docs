comparison expression

[Relational expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that joins two or more [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using a [comparison operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenrelational_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and in accordance with certain [comparison rules](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencomparison_rule_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
