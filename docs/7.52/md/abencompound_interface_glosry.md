Compound Interface

Interface in which at least one [component interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencomponent_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is included in its declaration with [INTERFACES](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapinterfaces_ifac.htm).
