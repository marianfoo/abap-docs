constructor

A special [method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmethod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that sets a defined initial value for the status ([attributes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenattribute_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) of the class or its [objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenobject_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). There is an [instance constructor](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninstance_constructor_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [instance attributes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninstance_attribute_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and a [static constructor](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenstatic_constructor_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [static attributes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenstatic_attribute_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The constructors always exist implicitly, but must still be declared explicitly for your own implementations. Special [constructor expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") also exist.
