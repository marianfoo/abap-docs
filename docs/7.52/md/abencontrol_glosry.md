control

Software component of the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used to handle complex [screen elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [Table control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentable_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")s, [tabstrip control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentabstrip_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")s, and [splitter control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensplitter_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")s are platform-independent controls processed directly using dynpro statements and [ABAP statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In addition, there are platform-independent [GUI controls](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abengui_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") accessed in ABAP by the [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [Control Framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencontrol_framework_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
