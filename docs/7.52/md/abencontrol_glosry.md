control

Software component of the [ABAP runtime environment](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") used to handle complex [screen elements](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [Table control](javascript:call_link\('abentable_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")s, [tabstrip control](javascript:call_link\('abentabstrip_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")s, and [splitter control](javascript:call_link\('abensplitter_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")s are platform-independent controls processed directly using dynpro statements and [ABAP statements](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In addition, there are platform-independent [GUI controls](javascript:call_link\('abengui_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") accessed in ABAP by the [classes](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [Control Framework](javascript:call_link\('abencontrol_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
