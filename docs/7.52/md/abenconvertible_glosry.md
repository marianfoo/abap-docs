convertible

Two different [data types](javascript:call_link\('abendata_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are convertible if [conversion rules](javascript:call_link\('abenconversion_rule_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") apply to them. Two [data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are convertible if their data types are convertible and their content allows the corresponding conversion. Data objects of [compatible](javascript:call_link\('abencompatible_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") data types are assigned to each other without conversion.
