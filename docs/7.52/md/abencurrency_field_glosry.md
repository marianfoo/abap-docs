currency field

Component of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or [view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenview_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of type CURR. It is used for entering an amount in a particular currency. The content of a currency field is interpreted as an integer in the smallest units of the currency. A currency field must be associated with a [currency key](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencurrency_key_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a structure, database table, or view that specifies the currency and number of decimal places.
