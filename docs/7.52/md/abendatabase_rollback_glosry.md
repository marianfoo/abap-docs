database rollback

End of a [database LUW](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), in which all changing database operations are cancelled until the LUW begins. In an [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), database rollbacks are triggered either implicitly, as well as als through explicit requirements.
