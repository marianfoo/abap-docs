database rollback

End of a [database LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), in which all changing database operations are cancelled until the LUW begins. In an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), database rollbacks are triggered either implicitly, as well as als through explicit requirements.
