deep

Property of a [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where the content of its [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is either a [reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenreference_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that points to the actual content ([reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")), or it is addressed internally using references ([internal tables,](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [strings](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenstring_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [boxed components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenboxed_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")). Also refer to [flat](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenflat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
