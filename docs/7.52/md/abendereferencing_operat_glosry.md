Dereferencing Operator

The special [operator](javascript:call_link\('abenoperator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") \->\* is used for dereferencing [data reference variables](javascript:call_link\('abendata_reference_variable_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") at [operand positions](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
