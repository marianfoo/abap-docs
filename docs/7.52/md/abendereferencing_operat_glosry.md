Dereferencing Operator

The special [operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") \->\* is used for dereferencing [data reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_reference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") at [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
