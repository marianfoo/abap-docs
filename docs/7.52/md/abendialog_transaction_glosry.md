dialog transaction

The [transaction code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentransaction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a dialog transaction is associated with a [dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). When the [transaction](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentransaction_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is called, the associated program is loaded and the dynpro called after the [event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenevent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [LOAD-OF-PROGRAM](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapload-of-program.htm).
