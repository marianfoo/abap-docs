DML

Data Manipulation Language. Subset of [SQL](javascript:call_link\('abensql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The statements of DML read and change the contents of [relational](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_database_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In ABAP, DML is represented using [Open SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenopen_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
