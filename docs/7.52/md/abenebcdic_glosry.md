EBCDIC

Abbreviation of Extended Binary Coded Decimal Interchange Code. An 8-bit [character set](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencharacter_set_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") based on [BCD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenbcd_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). EBCDIC covers mostly the same characters as [ASCII](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenascii_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
