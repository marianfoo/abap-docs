Elementary Data Type

[Data type](javascript:call_link\('abendata_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of fixed or variable length that is neither [structured](javascript:call_link\('abenstructured_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") nor a [table type](javascript:call_link\('abentable_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [reference type](javascript:call_link\('abenreference_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In particular, the [predefined ABAP types](javascript:call_link\('abenpredefined_data_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are elementary.
