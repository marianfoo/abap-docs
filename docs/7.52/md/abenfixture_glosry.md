fixture

Test configuration that produces unique test behavior. A fixture consists of test data, test objects, resources, and connections. A fixture is realized through the methods *\[*class\_*\]*setup and *\[*class\_*\]*teardown of [test classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentest_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Unit](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_unit_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"),
