fixture

Test configuration that produces unique test behavior. A fixture consists of test data, test objects, resources, and connections. A fixture is realized through the methods *\[*class\_*\]*setup and *\[*class\_*\]*teardown of [test classes](javascript:call_link\('abentest_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Unit](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_unit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"),
