Formatting Settings

Part of the [language environment](javascript:call_link\('abenlanguage_environment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") where the formatting for numbers, dates, and times is defined. Defined with either fixed values in the [user master record](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_master_record_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or by [country-specific formats](javascript:call_link\('abencountry_formats.htm'\)).
