functional method call

Call of a [functional method](javascript:call_link\('abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a suitable [reader position](javascript:call_link\('abenreading_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), where the [return value](javascript:call_link\('abenreturn_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the method is used as an [operand](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
