functional operand position

[Reading position](javascript:call_link\('abenreading_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with any [operand type](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), in which (alongside a suitable [data object](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) a [functional method](javascript:call_link\('abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [method chainings](javascript:call_link\('abenmethod_chaining_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a [constructor expression](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or a [table expression](javascript:call_link\('abentable_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") can be specified, the [return value](javascript:call_link\('abenreturn_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or result of which has a suitable [data type](javascript:call_link\('abendata_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
