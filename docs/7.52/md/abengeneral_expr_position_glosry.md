general expression position

[Reader position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenreading_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which (alongside a suitable [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) a [constructor expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [table expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentable_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [calculation expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencalculation_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [predefined function](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenpredefined_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [functional method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or a [method chaining](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmethod_chaining_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can also be specified.
