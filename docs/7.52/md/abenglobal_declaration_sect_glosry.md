global declaration section

Area following the opening statement of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), in which [interfaces](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninterface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are visible in the whole program can be declared.
