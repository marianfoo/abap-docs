SAP HANA Transport for ABAP

HTA for short. Infrastructure used as an interface between transports of database objects from the [SAP HANA database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenhana_database_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [Change and Transport System (CTS)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencts_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in ABAP.
