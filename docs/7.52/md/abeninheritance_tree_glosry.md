Inheritance tree

An [inheritance hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninheritance_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [single inheritance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensingle_inheritance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A unique path leads from each [subclass](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to a [root class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenroot_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). .
