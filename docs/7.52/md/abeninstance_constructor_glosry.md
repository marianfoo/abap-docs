instance constructor

[Constructor](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is declared under the name [constructor](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor.htm'\)) as an [instance method](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [class](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and which is automatically called when the class is [instantiated](javascript:call_link\('abeninstantiation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), after the generation of the [object](javascript:call_link\('abenobject_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
