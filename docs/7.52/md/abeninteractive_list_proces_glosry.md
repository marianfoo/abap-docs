interactive list processing

[List processing](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlist_processing_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that takes place after [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on [lists](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlist_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The associated [event keywords](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenevent_keyword_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are [AT LINE-SELECTION](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapat_line-selection.htm), [AT USER-COMMAND](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapat_user-command.htm), and [AT PFnn](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapat_pfnn.htm).
