join expression

Two or more [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") joined using the [language element](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [JOIN](javascript:call_link\('abapselect_join.htm'\)) in the [SELECT](javascript:call_link\('abapselect.htm'\)) statement in [Open SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenopen_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or the [SELECT](javascript:call_link\('abencds_f1_select_statement.htm'\)) statement in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
