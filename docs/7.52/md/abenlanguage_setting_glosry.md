language setting

Part of the [language environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlanguage_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The language setting of an ABAP program comprises the [logon language](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenlogon_language_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [text environment language](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentext_env_langu_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [text environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentext_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
