list dynpro

Predefined [dynpro](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") used to display [screen lists](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The list dynpro is a component of the [list processor](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_processor_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and is accessed either implicitly during processing of an [executable program](javascript:call_link\('abenexecutable_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or explicitly using a [LEAVE TO LIST-PROCESSING](javascript:call_link\('abapleave_to_list-processing.htm'\)) statement.
