local data

Local data are declared in [Procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and are only known there. Also see [global data](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenglobal_data_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
