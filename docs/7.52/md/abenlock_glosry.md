lock

Locks protect data from being accessed by multiple users at the same time, either as a [shared lock](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenshared_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or an [exclusive lock](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenexclusive_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See [database lock](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [SAP lock](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
