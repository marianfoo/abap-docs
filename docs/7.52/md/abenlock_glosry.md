lock

Locks protect data from being accessed by multiple users at the same time, either as a [shared lock](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or an [exclusive lock](javascript:call_link\('abenexclusive_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See [database lock](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [SAP lock](javascript:call_link\('abensap_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
