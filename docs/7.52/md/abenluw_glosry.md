LUW

Logical unit of work. Time between two consistent states on the [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See [SAP LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [database LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
