macro

A summarized string of statements for internal reuse of [source code](javascript:call_link\('abensource_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") within a program. In [ABAP source code](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), macros are defined between [DEFINE](javascript:call_link\('abapdefine.htm'\)) and [END-OF-DEFINITION](javascript:call_link\('abapend-of-definition.htm'\)).
