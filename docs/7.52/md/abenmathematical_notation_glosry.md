Mathematical Notation

Representation of a number as a digit sequence with possible [decimal separators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where the plus/minus sign is specified in front of the digits.
