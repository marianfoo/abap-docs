mesh association

[Association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenassociation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [mesh](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmesh_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Attribute of a [mesh node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmesh_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and therefore part of the [mesh type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmesh_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Associates mesh nodes as [mesh paths](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmesh_path_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
