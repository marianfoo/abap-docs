message maintenance

Tool in [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for creating and editing [messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenmessage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
