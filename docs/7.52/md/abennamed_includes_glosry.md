named includes

Grouping of [components](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") under one name. Instead of an [offset/length specification](javascript:call_link\('abenoffset_length_specific_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), subareas of a structure can be addressed by this name.
