obsolete statement

[ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that still exists in the language for reasons of downward compatibility, but that is no longer used. Most obsolete statements are permitted only outside [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
