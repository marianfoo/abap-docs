Offset

Position specification for a charater or a byte in a [character-](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [byte-type data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenbyte_like_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where counting begins wih 0. The value of an offset can also be understood as the identifier of the beginning of the line, the spacing between the characters or bytes, and the end of the line.
