Password

Sequence of characters that is only known to a specific [user](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and is required by this user to [log on](javascript:call_link\('abenlogon_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Together with the [user name](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_name_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), the password enables authorized use of an AS ABAP.
