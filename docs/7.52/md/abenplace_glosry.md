place in a decimal number

Place in a [decimal number](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendecimal_number_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Decimal places are both integer digits (before the decimal separator) and [decimal places](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenfractional_portion_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (after the decimal separator).
