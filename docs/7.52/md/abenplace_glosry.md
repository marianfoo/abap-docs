place in a decimal number

Place in a [decimal number](javascript:call_link\('abendecimal_number_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Decimal places are both integer digits (before the decimal separator) and [decimal places](javascript:call_link\('abenfractional_portion_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (after the decimal separator).
