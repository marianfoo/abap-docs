platform

The current system environment of an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), consisting of the operating system of the current [application server](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenapplication_server_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the [database system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendatabase_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the [text environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentext_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the communication interfaces.
