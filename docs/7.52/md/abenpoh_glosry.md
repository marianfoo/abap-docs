POH

Process On Help Request, [dynpro event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendynpro_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Triggered when the [field help](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenfield_help_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (F1) for an [input field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninput_output_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is requested.
