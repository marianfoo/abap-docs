POH

Process On Help Request, [dynpro event](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_event_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Triggered when the [field help](javascript:call_link\('abenfield_help_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (F1) for an [input field](javascript:call_link\('abeninput_output_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on the [screen](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is requested.
