Pragma

[Program directive](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenprogram_directive_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to the [ABAP Compiler](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_compiler_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [extended program check](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenextended_program_check_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to hide warnings from the [syntax check](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensyntax_check_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and messages of the extended check.
