predicate method

[Functional method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") whose [return code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenreturn_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") has the type abap\_bool from the [type group](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentype_group_1_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ABAP. A predicate method is particularly well suited to [predicative method calls](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenpredicative_method_call_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), but is not mandatory.
