report

The reading and displaying of data, for example in a [list](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Outdated term for an [executable program](javascript:call_link\('abenexecutable_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that performs [reporting](javascript:call_link\('abenreporting_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") tasks only.
