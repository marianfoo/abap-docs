SAP Basis

Before Release 6.10, SAP Basis was the central platform for all SAP applications written in ABAP. From Release 6.10, the functions of SAP Basis are fully integrated into [Application Server ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_personality_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
