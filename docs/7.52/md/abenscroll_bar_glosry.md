Component of [SAP GUI](javascript:call_link\('abensap_gui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Created automatically whenever a [screen](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [screen element](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is too large for the area available in the [GUI window](javascript:call_link\('abengui_window_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
