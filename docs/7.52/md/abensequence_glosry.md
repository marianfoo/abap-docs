sequence

[Control structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencontrol_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that consists of a [statement block](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenstatement_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not defined by keywords and is executed only once without a condition.
