static attribute

[Attribute](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenattribute_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") declared with [CLASS-DATA](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapclass-data.htm) of a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The attribute is valid independent of the [instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abeninstance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a class. The contents of the static attribute determine the instance-dependent state of a class.
