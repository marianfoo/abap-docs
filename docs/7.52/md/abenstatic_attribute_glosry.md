static attribute

[Attribute](javascript:call_link\('abenattribute_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") declared with [CLASS-DATA](javascript:call_link\('abapclass-data.htm'\)) of a [class](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The attribute is valid independent of the [instance](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a class. The contents of the static attribute determine the instance-dependent state of a class.
