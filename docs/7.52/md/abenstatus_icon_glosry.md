status icon

[Icon](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenicon_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") displayed as a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Is associated with a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the same name in the [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") must have the [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ICONS in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
