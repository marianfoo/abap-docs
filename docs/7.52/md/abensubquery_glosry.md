subquery

[Query](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenquery_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used in an [Open SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenopen_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") statement for subqueries. Subqueries are possible in [SQL conditions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensql_cond_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), for the definition of a [common table expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abencommon_table_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") after [WITH](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapwith.htm), or as a [data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapinsert_source.htm) of an [INSERT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abapinsert_dbtab.htm) statement.
