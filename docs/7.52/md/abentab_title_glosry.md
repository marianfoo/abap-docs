tab title

A single-line element located at the top of a [tab page](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentab_page_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and associated with a [function code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenfunction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A tab page is selected when the tab title is clicked.
