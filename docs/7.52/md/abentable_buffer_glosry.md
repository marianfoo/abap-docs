table buffer

Area in the [shared memory](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_memory_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") used for [table buffering](javascript:call_link\('abensap_buffering_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The table buffer is managed by the [database interface](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_interface_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
