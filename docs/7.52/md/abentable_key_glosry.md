Table Key

Columns of an [internal table](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or of a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") whose content identifies table rows. [Primary](javascript:call_link\('abenprimary_table_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [secondary table keys](javascript:call_link\('abensecondary_table_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are differentiated between for internal tables.
