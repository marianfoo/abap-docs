Tabstrip control

Tabstrip controls enable tab pages to be displayed on dynpros. A tabstrip control is a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") constructed from multiple [tab pages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentab_page_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or tabs.
