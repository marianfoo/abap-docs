text field literal

[Character literal](javascript:call_link\('abencharacter_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the type [text field](javascript:call_link\('abentext_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with the length of the specified characters. A text field literal can be associated with a [text symbol](javascript:call_link\('abentext_symbol_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
