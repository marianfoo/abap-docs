time stamp

Combined [date](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abendate_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")/ [time](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentime_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for identifying a point in time. Time stamps in the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are [POSIX time stamps](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenposix_timestamp_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") independent of time zone. Their [representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentime_stamp_oview.htm) is provided by [packed numbers](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenpacked_number_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined by the data types TIMESTAMP or TIMESTAMPL from [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
