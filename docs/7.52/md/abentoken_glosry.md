token

Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), into which the Compiler breaks down the statement. Tokens are are [ABAP words](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_word_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [operands](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [operators](javascript:call_link\('abenoperator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and some special characters.
