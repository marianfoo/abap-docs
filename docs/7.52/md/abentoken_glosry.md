token

Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), into which the Compiler breaks down the statement. Tokens are are [ABAP words](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_word_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and some special characters.
