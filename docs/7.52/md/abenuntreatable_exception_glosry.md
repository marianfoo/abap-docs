Untreatable Exception

[Exception](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is not assigned to any [exception class](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and which cannot be handled with [TRY - CATCH - ENDTRY](javascript:call_link\('abaptry.htm'\)) in the program. Untreatable exceptions are triggered by the [ABAP-runtime environment](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and usually occur as a reaction to errors that cannot be handled properly in the program. An untreatable exception always leads to a [runtime error](javascript:call_link\('abenruntime_error_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
