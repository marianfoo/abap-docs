UTC time stamp

[Time stamp](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abentime_stamp_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that references the [UTC](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenutc_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") reference time. The time stamps in the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are UTC time stamps, but because they are [POSIX time stamps](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenposix_timestamp_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") do not support [leap seconds](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenleap_second_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
