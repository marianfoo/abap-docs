UTF-8

8 bit [Unicode character representation](javascript:call_link\('abenunicode_char_represent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [ASCII](javascript:call_link\('abenascii_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") characters are represented by one byte. Other European characters are represented in 2 bytes. Most Asian characters are represented in 3 bytes. The characters in the [surrogate area](javascript:call_link\('abensurrogate_area_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are addressed directly using 4 bytes.
