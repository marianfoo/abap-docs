UTF-8

8 bit [Unicode character representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenunicode_char_represent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [ASCII](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenascii_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") characters are represented by one byte. Other European characters are represented in 2 bytes. Most Asian characters are represented in 3 bytes. The characters in the [surrogate area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensurrogate_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are addressed directly using 4 bytes.
