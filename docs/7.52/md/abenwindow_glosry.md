An area on a screen surrounded by a frame. A window in [SAP GUI](javascript:call_link\('abensap_gui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is known as a [GUI window](javascript:call_link\('abengui_window_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
