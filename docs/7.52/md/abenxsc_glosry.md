SAP HANA XSC

SAP HANA Extended Application Services Classic Model. Earlier version of [XS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenxs_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and predecessor to [XSC](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenxsa_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). XSC objects are saved to the [SAP HANA repository](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and developed and managed in [SAP HANA Studio](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_752_index_htm/7.52/en-US/abenhana_studio_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
