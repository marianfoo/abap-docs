MULTIPLY - Quick reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abapmultiply.htm)

Syntax

MULTIPLY dobj1 BY dobj2.

Effect

Multiplies the content of dobj1 with the content of dobj2 and assigns the result to dobj1.
