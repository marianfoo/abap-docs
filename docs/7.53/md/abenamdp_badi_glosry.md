AMDP BAdI

Special [BAdI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbadi_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [AMDP procedure implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). When the associated [AMDP procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is called from another [AMDP method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenamdp_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), this done on the basis of the switch-based rules of the [enhancement concept](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenenhancement_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
