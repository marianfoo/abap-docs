[Search help](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensearch_help_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that expands another search help.
