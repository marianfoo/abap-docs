append structure

[Structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") appended to another structure or [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and which appends their [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to them. Append structures can be added to structures and database tables delivered by SAP in customer systems, without making modifications.
