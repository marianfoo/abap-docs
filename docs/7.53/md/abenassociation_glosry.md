association

Relationships between the nodes of a hierarchy. Can be either [CDS associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [mesh associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenmesh_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
