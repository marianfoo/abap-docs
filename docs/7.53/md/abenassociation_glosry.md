association

Relationships between the nodes of a hierarchy. Can be either [CDS associations](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [mesh associations](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
