bit operator

Is applied to an [operand](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or joins two operands of a [bit expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbit_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The possible bit operators are [BIT-NOT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbit_operators.htm), [BIT-AND](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbit_operators.htm), [BIT-OR](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbit_operators.htm), and [BIT-XOR](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbit_operators.htm).
