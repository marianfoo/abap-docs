business function

Abbreviated as BF. [Repository object](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that represents a self-contained (business) function within the scope of [Switch Framework](javascript:call_link\('abenswitch_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Multiple business functions are pooled to form a [Business Function Set](javascript:call_link\('abenbusiness_function_set_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Technically, a business function is a pool of [switches](javascript:call_link\('abenswitch_german_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are part of the same function. When a business function is switched on, the associated switches are switched on too. Business functions are created in [transaction](javascript:call_link\('abentransaction_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") SFW2 and switched in transaction SFW5.
