character-like expression position

[Character-like](javascript:call_link\('abencharlike_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [reading position](javascript:call_link\('abenreading_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in which, in addition to a [character-like data object](javascript:call_link\('abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that can be converted to the type of the operand, a [character-like expression](javascript:call_link\('abencharlike_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") can also be specified.
