commercial notation

Displays a number as a string of digits, with a [decimal separator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") if applicable, with the plus or minus sign displayed after the digits.
