composite enhancement implementation

Semantic combination of ([simple](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensimple_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or other composite) [enhancement implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenenhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the aim of providing structure.
