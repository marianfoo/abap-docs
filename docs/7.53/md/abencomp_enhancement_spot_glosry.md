composite enhancement spot

Semantic combination of [simple](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensimple_enhancement_spot_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and other composite [enhancement spots](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenenhancement_spot_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the aim of providing structure.
