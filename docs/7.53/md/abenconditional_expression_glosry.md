conditional expression

[Constructor expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using a [conditional operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenconditional_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that creates a value or raises a [class-based exception](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenclass_based_exception_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as specified by a [logical expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [case distinction](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencase_distinction_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
