context

Environment of an [object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenobject_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Determines its visibility and retention period. Possible contexts are [procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [instances](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abeninstance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), classes, and [programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
