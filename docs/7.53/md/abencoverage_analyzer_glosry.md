Coverage Analyzer

Tool for recording how often programs or individual [processing blocks](javascript:call_link\('abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are executed, classified by time period, [user](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or subject area. It is called using [transaction code](javascript:call_link\('abentransaction_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") SCOV.
