database field

Smallest logical unit of the [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The properties of a database field are defined in [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") by a [data element](javascript:call_link\('abendata_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
