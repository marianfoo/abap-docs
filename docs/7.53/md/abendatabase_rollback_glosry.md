database rollback

End of a [database LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), when all modifying database operations are canceled until the start of the LUW. In [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), database rollbacks are triggered both implicitly and when certain explicit requirements are met.
