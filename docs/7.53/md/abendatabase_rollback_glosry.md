database rollback

End of a [database LUW](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), when all modifying database operations are canceled until the start of the LUW. In [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), database rollbacks are triggered both implicitly and when certain explicit requirements are met.
