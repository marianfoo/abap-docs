DML

Data Manipulation Language. Subset of [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The statements of DML read and change the contents of [relational](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenrelational_database_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In ABAP, DML is represented using [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenopen_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
