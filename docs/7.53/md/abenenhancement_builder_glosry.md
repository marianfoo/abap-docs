Enhancement Builder

Tool in [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used to create and manage [enhancements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenenhancement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") within the [enhancement concept](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenenhancement_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
