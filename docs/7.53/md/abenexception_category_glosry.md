exception category

Categorization of [class-based exceptions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenclass_based_exception_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in accordance with the [superclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensuperclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of their [exception classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenexception_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The exception category determines how exceptions need to be declared in [procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
