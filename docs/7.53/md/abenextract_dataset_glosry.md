extract dataset

Structured unnamed dataset of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Consists of rows that are organized as [field groups](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenfield_group_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Not part of the ABAP type concept for [data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
