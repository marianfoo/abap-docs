decimal place

Also called fractional digit. Decimal places contain the fractional part of a number and are shown on the right of the separator, regardless of the number system. In the case of [decimal numbers](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendecimal_number_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the decimal places are the [places](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenplace_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that come after the [decimal separator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
