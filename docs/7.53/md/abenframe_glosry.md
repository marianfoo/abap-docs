frame

[Screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used to group other screen elements and on which no [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are possible. A frame is not associated with a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
