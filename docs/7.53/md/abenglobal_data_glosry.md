global data

[Data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are known in the entire ABAP program. Global data is declared in the [global declaration part](javascript:call_link\('abenglobal_declaration_sect_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a program. Also see [local data](javascript:call_link\('abenlocal_data_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
