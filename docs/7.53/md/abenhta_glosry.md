SAP HANA Transport for ABAP

HTA for short. Infrastructure that provides an interface between transports of database objects in the [SAP HANA database](javascript:call_link\('abenhana_database_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([SAP HANA Repository](javascript:call_link\('abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") objects and [HDI objects](javascript:call_link\('abenhdi_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) and the [Change and Transport System (CTS)](javascript:call_link\('abencts_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in ABAP.
