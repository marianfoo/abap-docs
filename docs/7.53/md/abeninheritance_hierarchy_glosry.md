inheritance hierarchy

Hierarchical relationship between [superclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensuperclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [subclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [inheritance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abeninheritance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") functions. In [single inheritance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensingle_inheritance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the inheritance hierarchy forms an [inheritance tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abeninheritance_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
