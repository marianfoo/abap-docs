join expression

Two or more [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") joined using the [language element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [JOIN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abapselect_join.htm) in the [SELECT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abapselect.htm) statement in [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenopen_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or the [SELECT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencds_f1_select_statement.htm) statement in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
