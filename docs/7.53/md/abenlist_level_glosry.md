list level

Position of a [list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenlist_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [list buffer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenlist_buffer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A [basic list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenbasic_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") has the [list index](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenlist_index_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") 0; the list index of a [details list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendetails_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is greater than 0. Content of the [system field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensystem_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [sy-lsind](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensystem_fields.htm).
