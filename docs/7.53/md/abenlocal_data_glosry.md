local data

Local data is declared in [procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and is are only known there. See also [global data](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenglobal_data_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
