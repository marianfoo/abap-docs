maintenance view

Special [classic view](javascript:call_link\('abenclassical_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for multiple [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A maintenance view is not defined as an [SQL view](javascript:call_link\('abensql_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on the [database](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
