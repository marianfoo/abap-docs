mesh association

[Association](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [mesh](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Attribute of a [mesh node](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and therefore part of the [mesh type](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Associates mesh nodes as [mesh paths](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_path_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
