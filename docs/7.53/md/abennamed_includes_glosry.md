named includes

Grouping of [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") under one name. Instead of an [offset/length specification](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenoffset_length_specific_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), subareas of a structure can be addressed by this name.
