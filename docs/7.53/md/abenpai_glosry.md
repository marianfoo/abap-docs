PAI

Process After Input, [dynpro event](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_event_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Triggered by a [user action](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_action_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on the [GUI](javascript:call_link\('abengui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). At PAI time, the contents of [dynpro fields](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are passed to [data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the same name in the [ABAP program](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
