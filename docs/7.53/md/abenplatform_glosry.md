platform

Current system environment of an [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), consisting of the operating system of the [host computer](javascript:call_link\('abenhost_computer_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the current [AS Instance](javascript:call_link\('abenapplication_server_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), the [database system](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_system_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), the [text environment](javascript:call_link\('abentext_environment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and the communication interfaces.
