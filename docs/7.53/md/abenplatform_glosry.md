platform

Current system environment of an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), consisting of the operating system of the [host computer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenhost_computer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the current [AS Instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenapplication_server_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the [database system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the [text environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abentext_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and the communication interfaces.
