reader stream

Also called [input stream](javascript:call_link\('abeninput_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A [data stream](javascript:call_link\('abendata_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") associated with a [data source](javascript:call_link\('abendata_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [filter stream](javascript:call_link\('abenfilter_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that reads data from another stream . The direction of a reader stream is always away from the data source. Data can only be taken from a reader stream.
