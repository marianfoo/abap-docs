redefinition

Term in [ABAP Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_objects_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A redefinition enables an [instance method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abeninstance_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [subclass](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to be reimplemented without the [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abeninterface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") being changed.
