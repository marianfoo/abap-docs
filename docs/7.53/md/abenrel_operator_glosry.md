relational operator

Umbrella term for [comparison operators](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [predicate operators](javascript:call_link\('abenpredicate_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators occur in [relational expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators are a type of [logical operator](javascript:call_link\('abenlogical_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
