Repository Info System

Called from [Object Navigator](javascript:call_link\('abenobject_navigator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [transaction code](javascript:call_link\('abentransaction_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") SE15.
