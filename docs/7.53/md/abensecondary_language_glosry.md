secondary language

Language specified in the [profile parameter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenprofile_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") zcsa/second\_language. The secondary language is used for [messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenmessage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [text pools](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abentext_pool_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and translatable [CDS annotations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in cases where the required language is not found.
