single inheritance

[Inheritance](javascript:call_link\('abeninheritance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") method where, unlike multiple inheritance, a [class](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") can have multiple [subclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensubclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") but only one direct [superclass](javascript:call_link\('abensuperclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") See also [inheritance hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abeninheritance_hierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
