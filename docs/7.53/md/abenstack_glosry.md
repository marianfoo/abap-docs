stack

Part of the [program memory](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenroll_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which [named data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abennamed_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of programs and [procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are stacked. The memory used by a procedure is released again once the procedure finishes. See also [heap](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenheap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
