structured type

[Data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a structure are themselves data types.
