subscreen area

Area within a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which other screens can be represented as [subscreens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensubscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
