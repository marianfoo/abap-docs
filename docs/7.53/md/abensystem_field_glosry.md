system field

Predefined [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can be filled in accordance with the context by the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and analyzed in the program.
