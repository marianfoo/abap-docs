tab title

A single-line element located at the top of a [tab page](javascript:call_link\('abentab_page_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and associated with a [function code](javascript:call_link\('abenfunction_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A tab page is selected when the tab title is clicked.
