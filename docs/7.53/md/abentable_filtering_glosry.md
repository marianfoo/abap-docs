table filtering

Creates the content of an [internal table](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") by filtering the rows of an internal table in a [constructor expression](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with the [filter operator](javascript:call_link\('abenfilter_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [FILTER](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor_expression_filter.htm'\)).
