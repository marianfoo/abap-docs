table sharing

[Sharing](javascript:call_link\('abensharing_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [internal tables](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [assignments](javascript:call_link\('abenassignment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [pass by value](javascript:call_link\('abenpass_by_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Table sharing only occurs between tables of the same category if their [row types](javascript:call_link\('abenrow_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") do not themselves contain [table types](javascript:call_link\('abentable_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
