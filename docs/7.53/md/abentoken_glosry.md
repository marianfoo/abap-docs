token

Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The compiler breaks down statements into tokens. Possible tokens are [ABAP words](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_word_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and certain special characters.
