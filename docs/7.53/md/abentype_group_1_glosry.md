type group

[ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), administered by [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), that is initiated by the statement [TYPE-POOL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abaptype-pool.htm) and which contains the definitions of globally visible [data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [constants](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenconstant_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") , plus [macros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenmacro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") . A type group is loaded the first time its data types and constants are used. It does not support its own [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
