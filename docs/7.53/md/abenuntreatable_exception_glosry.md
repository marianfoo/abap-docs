non-handleable exception

[Exception](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") not associated with an [exception class](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and that cannot be handled in the program by [TRY - CATCH - ENDTRY](javascript:call_link\('abaptry.htm'\)). Non-handleable exceptions are raised by the [ABAP runtime environment](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and are usually a response to error situations that cannot be handled effectively in a program. A non-handleable exception always produces a [runtime error](javascript:call_link\('abenruntime_error_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
