UUID

Universal Unique Identifier. Universally unique identifier, generated in accordance with [OSF standards](javascript:call_link\('http://www.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/9629399/apdxa.htm'\)) from the system time stamp and the network address. The [system class](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") CL\_SYSTEM\_UUID is used to do this.
