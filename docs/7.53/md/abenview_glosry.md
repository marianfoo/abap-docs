view

Grouping of columns of one or more [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or other [database objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendb_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in accordance with an application-specific view. In [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), views are defined in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and can be referenced as [data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (like [flat structures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenflat_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")). Views can be used like database tables in reading [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenopen_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") statements. ABAP Dictionary uses [classic views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenclassical_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [CDS views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abencds_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [external views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abenexternal_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
