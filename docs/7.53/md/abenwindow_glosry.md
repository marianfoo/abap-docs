window

An area on a screen surrounded by a frame. A window in [SAP GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abensap_gui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is known as a [GUI window](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_753_index_htm/7.53/en-US/abengui_window_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
