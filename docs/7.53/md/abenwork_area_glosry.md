work area

Name for a [data object](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") used mostly when working with [internal tables](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as a source for modifying operations or as a target for reading operations.
