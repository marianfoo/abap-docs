SAP HANA XSC

SAP HANA Extended Application Services Classic Model. Earlier version of [XS](javascript:call_link\('abenxs_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and predecessor to [XSC](javascript:call_link\('abenxsa_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). XSC objects are saved to the [SAP HANA repository](javascript:call_link\('abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and are usually developed and managed in [SAP HANA Studio](javascript:call_link\('abenhana_studio_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
