DIVIDE - Quick reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abapsubtract_multiply_divide.htm)

Syntax

DIVIDE dobj1 BY dobj2.

Effect

Obsolete: Divides the content of dobj1 by the content of dobj2 and assigns the result dobj1.
