SUBTRACT-CORRESPONDING - Quick reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abapadd-corresponding.htm)

Syntax

SUBTRACT-CORRESPONDING struc1 FROM struc2.

Effect

Obsolete: Subtracts the components of a structure struc1 from the corresponding component of a structure struc2.
