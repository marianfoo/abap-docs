ABAP keyword

[ABAP word](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_word_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that introduces an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In combination with other ABAP words or [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), it defines an [ABAP language element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
