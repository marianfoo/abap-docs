ABAP Managed Database Procedures

Abbreviated as AMDP. Class-based framework for managing and calling [stored procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenstored_procedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [database procedures](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_procedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as [AMDP procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenamdp_procedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
