ABAP system

An [ABAP Platform](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_platform_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") implemented using a [client-server system](javascript:call_link\('abenclient_server_system_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") consisting of

-   ABAP-specific components of the [presentation layer](javascript:call_link\('abenpresentation_layer_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") , such as [SAP GUI](javascript:call_link\('abensap_gui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") .

-   [Application Server ABAP (AS ABAP)](javascript:call_link\('abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")

-   [ABAP database schema](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_db_schema_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [standard AS ABAP database](javascript:call_link\('abenstandard_db_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") containing [system programs](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [system tables](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") plus [application programs](javascript:call_link\('abenapplication_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [application tables](javascript:call_link\('abenapplication_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

The name of an ABAP system matches the [system ID](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_id_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of its AS ABAP.
