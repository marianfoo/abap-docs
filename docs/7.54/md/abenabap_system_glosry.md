ABAP system

An [ABAP Platform](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_platform_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") implemented using a [client-server system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenclient_server_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") consisting of

-   ABAP-specific components of the [presentation layer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenpresentation_layer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") , such as [SAP GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensap_gui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") .

-   [Application Server ABAP (AS ABAP)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")

-   [ABAP database schema](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_db_schema_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [standard AS ABAP database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenstandard_db_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") containing [system programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensystem_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [system tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensystem_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") plus [application programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenapplication_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [application tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenapplication_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

The name of an ABAP system matches the [system ID](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensystem_id_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of its AS ABAP.
