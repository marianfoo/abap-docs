ancestor node

The ancestor nodes of a [child node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenchild_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are its [parent nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenparent_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (and their parent nodes, and so on).
