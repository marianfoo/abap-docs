append structure

[Structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") appended to another structure or [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and which appends their [components](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to them. Append structures can be added to structures and database tables delivered by SAP in customer systems, without making modifications.
