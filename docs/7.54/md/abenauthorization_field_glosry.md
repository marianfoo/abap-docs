authorization field

Smallest unit of an [authorization object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenauthorization_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). An authorization field represents either data such as a [key field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenkey_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or activities such as reading or changing. Activities are specified as identifiers stored in the database table TACT and, for customers, in TACTZ.
