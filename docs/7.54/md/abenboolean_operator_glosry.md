Boolean operator

Negates or joins [logical expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The possible Boolean operators are [NOT](javascript:call_link\('abenlogexp_not.htm'\)), [AND](javascript:call_link\('abenlogexp_and.htm'\)), [OR](javascript:call_link\('abenlogexp_or.htm'\)), and [EQUIV](javascript:call_link\('abenlogexp_equiv.htm'\)). Boolean operators are a type of [logical operator](javascript:call_link\('abenlogical_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
