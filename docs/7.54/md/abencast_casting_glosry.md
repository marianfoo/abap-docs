casting

Handling of a [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by assuming a certain [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Explicit casting is possible using the statement [ASSIGN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abapassign.htm), using the addition [ASSIGNING](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abapread_table_outdesc.htm) when editing internal tables, using various assignments of [actual parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenactual_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to [formal parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and as [down cast](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendown_cast_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")s or [up cast](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenup_cast_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")s in the [assignments](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenassignment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") between [reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). An implicit casting sometimes takes place during the handling of [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") at certain [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
