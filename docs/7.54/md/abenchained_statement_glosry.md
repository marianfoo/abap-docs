chained statement

Abbreviated way of writing multiple [ABAP-statements](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that have the same initial part. The initial part is noted once and closed used a colon (:). The remaining parts follow, separated by commas.
