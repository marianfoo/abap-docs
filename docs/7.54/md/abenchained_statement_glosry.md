chained statement

Abbreviated way of writing multiple [ABAP-statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that have the same initial part. The initial part is noted once and closed used a colon (:). The remaining parts follow, separated by commas.
