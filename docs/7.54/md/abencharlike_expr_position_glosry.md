character-like expression position

[Character-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [reading position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreading_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which not just [character-like data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") but also [character-like expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be specified.
