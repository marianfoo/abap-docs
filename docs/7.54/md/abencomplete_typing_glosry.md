complete typing

[Typing](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentyping_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which the [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [formal parameter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [field symbol](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenfield_symbol_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is completely defined and which is valid when the access is dynamic or static.
