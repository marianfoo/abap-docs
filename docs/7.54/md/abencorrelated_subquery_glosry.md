correlated subquery

[Subquery](javascript:call_link\('abensubquery_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that uses columns of the wrapper [query](javascript:call_link\('abenquery_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in its [SQL conditions](javascript:call_link\('abensql_cond_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). It must be possible to assign the column uniquely to a [data source](javascript:call_link\('abendata_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the wrapper query. A correlated subquery is evaluated for each individual row of the results set in the wrapper query.
