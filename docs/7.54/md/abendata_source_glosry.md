data source

-   In a [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenopen_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [query](javascript:call_link\('abenquery_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a query in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a data source is a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") , a [classic view](javascript:call_link\('abenclassical_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a different resource such as a [common table expression](javascript:call_link\('abencommon_table_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a [hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a [table function](javascript:call_link\('abentable_function_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or an [internal table](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

-   In a [streaming](javascript:call_link\('abenstreaming_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") context, a data source is a repository from which a reader [data stream](javascript:call_link\('abendata_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") takes its data. A data source can be an external resource such as an [LOB](javascript:call_link\('abenlob_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") column of a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [file](javascript:call_link\('abenfile_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or an internal resource such as a [string](javascript:call_link\('abenstring_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or an [internal table](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [Streaming](javascript:call_link\('abenstreaming_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is currently implemented for [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [strings](javascript:call_link\('abenstring_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and [internal tables](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
