data stream

[Stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenstream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") connected directly to a [data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a [reader stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreader_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or connected directly to a [data sink](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_sink_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a [writer stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenwriter_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
