database field

Smallest logical unit of the [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The properties of a database field are defined in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by a [data element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
