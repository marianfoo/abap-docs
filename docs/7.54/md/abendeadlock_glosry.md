deadlock

Blocked access to shared files (such as a [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) due to errors in the lock concept (preventing competing access) or incorrect application of a lock concept.
