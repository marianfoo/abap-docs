end of line comment

Section of a program line, following an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or part of a statement, that is identified as a [comment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencomment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by double quotation marks (").
