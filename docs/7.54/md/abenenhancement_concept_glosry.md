enhancement concept

Also called Enhancement Framework. Framework for the integration of various concepts dealing with the [enhancement](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [repository objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The enhancement concept is realized by the [Enhancement Builder](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_builder_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") tool in [ABAP Workbench](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and in the case of [ABAP source code](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), by using specific [ABAP statements](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_framework.htm'\)).
