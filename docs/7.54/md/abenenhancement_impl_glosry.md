enhancement implementation

[Repository object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used for the administration of [enhancements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenenhancement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of other repository objects. Enhancement implementations are components of a hierarchical tree structure where the branches represent [compound](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencomp_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") enhancement implementations, and the leaves are [simple enhancement implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensimple_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
