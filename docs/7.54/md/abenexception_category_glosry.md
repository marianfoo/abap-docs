exception category

Categorization of [class-based exceptions](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_based_exception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in accordance with the [superclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensuperclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of their [exception classes](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The exception category determines how exceptions need to be declared in [procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenprocedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
