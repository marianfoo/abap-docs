foreign key field

Field in a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to which a [check table](javascript:call_link\('abencheck_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is assigned. Part of the [foreign key](javascript:call_link\('abenforeign_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [foreign key table](javascript:call_link\('abenforeign_key_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
