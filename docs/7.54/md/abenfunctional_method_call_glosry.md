functional method call

Call of a [functional method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a suitable [reading position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreading_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), where the [return value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreturn_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the method is used as an [operand](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
