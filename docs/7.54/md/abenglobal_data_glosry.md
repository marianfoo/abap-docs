global data

[Data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are known in the entire ABAP program. Global data is declared in the [global declaration part](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenglobal_declaration_sect_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a program. Also see [local data](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenlocal_data_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
