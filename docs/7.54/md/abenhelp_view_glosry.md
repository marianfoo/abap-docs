help view

Special [classic view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenclassical_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for multiple [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A maintenance view is not defined as an [SQL view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensql_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
