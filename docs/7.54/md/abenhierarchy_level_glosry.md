hierarchy level

Level of a [hierarchy node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [hierarchy column](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_column_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [HIERARCHY\_LEVEL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenddddl_hierarchy.htm) contains the value of the hierarchy level of a hierarchy node.
