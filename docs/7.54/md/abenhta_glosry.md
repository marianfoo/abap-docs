SAP HANA Transport for ABAP

HTA for short. Infrastructure that provides an interface between transports of database objects in the [SAP HANA database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhana_database_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ([SAP HANA Repository](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") objects and [HDI objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhdi_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) and the [Change and Transport System (CTS)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencts_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in ABAP.
