Late Numbering

A concept where new entity instances are given their final key shortly before saving in the database. Until this moment, the business logic uses a temporary key that has to be replaced when the [business object](javascript:call_link\('abenbusiness_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") data is saved.
