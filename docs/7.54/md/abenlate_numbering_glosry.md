Late Numbering

A concept where new entity instances are given their final key shortly before saving in the database. Until this moment, the business logic uses a temporary key that has to be replaced when the [business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenbusiness_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") data is saved.
