list

Classic list, medium used for the structured and formatted output of data. List output is either written to a [list buffer](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_buffer_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as a [screen list](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and displayed on a [list dynpro](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_dynpro_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or is sent to the [SAP spool system](javascript:call_link\('abensap_spool_system_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as a [spool list](javascript:call_link\('abenprint_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
