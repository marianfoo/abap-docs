logical operator

[Operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [logical expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Either a [Boolean operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenboolean_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for joining or negating logical expressions or a [relational operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrel_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [relational expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
