mathematical notation

Representation of a number as a string of digits (possibly with [decimal separators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) where the sign is placed in front of the digits.
