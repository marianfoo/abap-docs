member loop

Loop across the members of a group in a [group loop](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abengroup_loop_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") introduced using the statement [LOOP AT GROUP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abaploop_at_group.htm).
