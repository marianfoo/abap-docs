mesh

Instance of a [mesh type](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Special [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") whose [components](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), called [mesh nodes](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are exclusively [internal tables](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") associated with each other using [mesh associations](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
