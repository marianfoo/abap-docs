numeric text field

[Character-like data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for saving [numeric texts](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abennumeric_text_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with fixed lengths or a similar [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The associated [built-in ABAP type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenpredefined_abap_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is [n](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenbuiltin_types_character.htm). The internal representation used is the [character representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenchar_representation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the current [code page](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencodepage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
