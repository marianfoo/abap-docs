Statements for Implementing Methods

The functions of classes are implemented in methods, between METHOD - ENDMETHOD.

The statements allowed in methods include all parts of the ABAP language allowed in all procedures. It must be noted, however, that all statements in methods are subject to the [stricter syntax](javascript:call_link\('abenoo_differences_obsolete.htm'\)) of ABAP Objects.
