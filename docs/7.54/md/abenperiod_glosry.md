period

Time interval whose limits are defined by the content of the columns of a [temporal hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abentemporal_hierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). These columns are defined when the [hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is created.
