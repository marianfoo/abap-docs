period

Time interval whose limits are defined by the content of the columns of a [temporal hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentemporal_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). These columns are defined when the [hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is created.
