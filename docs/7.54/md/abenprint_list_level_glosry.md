spool list level

Hierarchical level of a stacked [spool list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenprint_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A spool list level has exactly one [spool request](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenspool_request_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") assigned to it.
