Push Channel Protocol

Abbreviated as PCP. Describes an SAP communications format used to exchange messages between programs and internal or external interfaces. At present, PCP is used by [ABAP Channels](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_channel_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [ABAP Daemons](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_daemon_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
