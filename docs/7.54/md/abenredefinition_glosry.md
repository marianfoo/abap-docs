redefinition

Term in [ABAP Objects](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_objects_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A redefinition enables an [instance method](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [subclass](javascript:call_link\('abensubclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to be reimplemented without the [interface](javascript:call_link\('abeninterface_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") being changed.
