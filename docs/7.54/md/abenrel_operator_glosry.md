relational operator

Umbrella term for [comparison operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrelational_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [predicate operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenpredicate_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators occur in [relational expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators are a type of [logical operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenlogical_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
