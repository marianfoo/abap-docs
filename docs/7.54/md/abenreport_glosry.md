report

The reading and displaying of data, for example in a [list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenlist_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Outdated term for an [executable program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenexecutable_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that performs [reporting](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreporting_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") tasks only.
