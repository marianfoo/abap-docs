sash

Divides a [splitter control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensplitter_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") into two [subscreen areas](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensubscreen_arrea_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
