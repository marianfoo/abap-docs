scalar subquery

[Subquery](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensubquery_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with one-column results set. The [SELECT list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abapselect_list.htm) of the subquery contains one element only.
