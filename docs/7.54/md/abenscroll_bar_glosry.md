scroll bar

Component of [SAP GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensap_gui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Created automatically whenever a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is too large for the area available in the [GUI window](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abengui_window_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
