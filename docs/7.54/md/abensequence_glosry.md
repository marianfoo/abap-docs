sequence

Also known as a series. [Control structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencontrol_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") consisting of a [statement block](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenstatement_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not defined by keywords and that is executed only once without a condition.
