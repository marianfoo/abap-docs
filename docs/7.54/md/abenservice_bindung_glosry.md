Business Service Binding

Short: Service Binding

Business service binding is an ABAP repository object used to bind a [service definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenrpm_service_definition.htm) to a client-server communication protocol, such as OData.
