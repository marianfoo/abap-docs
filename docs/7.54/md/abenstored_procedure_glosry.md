stored procedure

[Database procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_procedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Procedural [database object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendb_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") implemented and called specific to the platform. A stored procedure generally groups sequences of multiple [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") statements and contains further statements for controlling the sequence. [ABAP-managed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_managed_db_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") stored procedures are [AMDP procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [AMDP functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenamdp_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
