structured type

[Data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a structure are themselves data types.
