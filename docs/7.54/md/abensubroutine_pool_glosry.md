subroutine pool

[ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") usually containing [subroutines](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensubroutine_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") called from other ABAP programs. Does not support its own [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
