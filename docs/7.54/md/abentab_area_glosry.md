tab area

Area of a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on which [tabstrips](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentabstrip_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be displayed.
