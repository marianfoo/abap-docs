text field

In ABAP, [character-like data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for saving [character strings](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharacter_string_1_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with fixed lengths or a similar [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The associated [built-in ABAP type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenpredefined_abap_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is [c](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenbuiltin_types_character.htm). The internal representation used is the [character representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenchar_representation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the current [code page](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencodepage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

In [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that serves the sole purpose of displaying text and on which no [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are possible. A text field of a dynpro is not associated with a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
