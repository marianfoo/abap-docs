time-stamp-like expression position

[Reading position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenreading_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which, as well as a [time stamp field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentimestamp_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), an [expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abencharlike_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be specified whose result has a [time stamp type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentimestamp_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
