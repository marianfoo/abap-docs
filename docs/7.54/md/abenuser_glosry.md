user

1\. Defined in [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abensap_nw_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by a [user name](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenuser_name_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and a [user master record](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenuser_master_record_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [transaction code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentransaction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") SU01 .

2\. User (person or other system) that is logged on to the AS ABAP.

Once users have logged on to AS ABAP and have been authenticated, they can access functions and objects in AS ABAP during their [user session](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenuser_session_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as defined by their [authorizations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenauthorization_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
