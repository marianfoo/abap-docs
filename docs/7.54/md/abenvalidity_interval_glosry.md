validity interval

Time interval assigned to every [hierarchy node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [temporal hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abentemporal_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The validity interval is the intersection of the validity interval of the [ancestor node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenancestor_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [period](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abenperiod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the current node.
