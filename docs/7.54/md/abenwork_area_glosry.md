work area

Name for a [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used mostly when working with [internal tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_754_index_htm/7.54/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a source for modifying operations or as a target for reading operations.
