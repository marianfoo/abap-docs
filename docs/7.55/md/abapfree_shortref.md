FREE - Quick reference

[Reference](javascript:call_link\('abapfree_dataobject.htm'\))

Syntax

FREE dobj.

Effect

Initializes a data object dobj by assigning the type-dependent [initial value](javascript:call_link\('abeninitial_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In the case of internal tables, the entire memory area occupied by lines is freed.
