MULTIPLY - Quick reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapsubtract_multiply_divide.htm)

Syntax

MULTIPLY dobj1 BY dobj2.

Effect

Obsolete: Multiplies the content of dobj1 with the content of dobj2 and assigns the result to dobj1.
