ABP handler class

[Local class](javascript:call_link\('abenlocal_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP behavior pool (ABP)](javascript:call_link\('abenbehavior_pool_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is part of the [ABAP behavior implementation](javascript:call_link\('abenbehavior_implement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A handler class implements the [CRUD](javascript:call_link\('abencrud_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") operations defined in the business object's [CDS behavior definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
