action

A change operation that deviates from the default and that is assigned to a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

Actions can either have an instance reference (standard case) or are static (using the addition static).
