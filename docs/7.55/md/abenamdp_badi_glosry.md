AMDP BAdI

Special [BAdI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbadi_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [AMDP procedure implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). If the associated [AMDP procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is called from a different [AMDP method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenamdp_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), this takes place based on the switch-based rules of the [enhancement concept](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenenhancement_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
