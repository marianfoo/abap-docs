append structure

[Structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is appended to a different DDIC structure or [DDIC database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") thus enhancing its [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Structures and database tables delivered by SAP can be enhanced in customer systems by append structures without making modifications.
