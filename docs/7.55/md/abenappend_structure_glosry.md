append structure

[Structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is appended to a different DDIC structure or [DDIC database table](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") thus enhancing its [components](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Structures and database tables delivered by SAP can be enhanced in customer systems by append structures without making modifications.
