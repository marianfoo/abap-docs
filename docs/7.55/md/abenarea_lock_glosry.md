area lock

[Read lock](javascript:call_link\('abenread_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [change lock](javascript:call_link\('abenchange_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([write lock](javascript:call_link\('abenwrite_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [update lock](javascript:call_link\('abenupdate_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) on an [area instance version](javascript:call_link\('abenarea_instance_version_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [shared objects memory](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_objects_memory_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Area locks are set by attaching [area handles](javascript:call_link\('abenarea_handle_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to area instance versions.
