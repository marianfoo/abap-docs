authorization field

Smallest unit of an [authorization object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenauthorization_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). An authorization field represents either data such as a [key field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenkey_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [DDIC database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or activities such as reading or changing. Activities are specified as abbreviations that are stored in the DDIC database table TACT and in TACTZ for customers.
