basic arithmetic operation

The four basic arithmetic operation types addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division are implemented in [arithmetic expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarithmetic_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and in [calculation assignments](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencalculation_assignments.htm) by the [arithmetic operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarithmetic_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ([+](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\-](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\*](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), and [/](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm)).
