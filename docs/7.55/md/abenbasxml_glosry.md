basXML

Binary ABAP Serialization XML. Representation of [asXML](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenasxml_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [Binary XML](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbinary_xml_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). basXML is used, among other things, as an [RFC protocol](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrfc_protocol.htm) with the same name.
