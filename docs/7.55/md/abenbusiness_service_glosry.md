Business Service

BS for short. A business service is a [RESTful](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrestful_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") service in [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It consists of a [CDS service definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_service_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and a [service binding](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenservice_binding_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
