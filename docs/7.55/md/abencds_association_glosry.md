CDS association

[Association](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). CDS associations can be defined for [CDS view entities](javascript:call_link\('abencds_v2_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [CDS DDIC-based views](javascript:call_link\('abencds_v1_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [CDS projection views](javascript:call_link\('abencds_projection_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and [CDS abstract entities](javascript:call_link\('abencds_abstract_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") using the addition ASSOCIATION in the [CDS DDL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Associations define [join](javascript:call_link\('abenjoin_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") relationships between [association sources](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [association targets](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). They can be evaluated in [CDS DDL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [CDS DCL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_dcl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and in [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") using [SQL path expressions](javascript:call_link\('abensql_path_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [CDS compositions](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [to-parent associations](javascript:call_link\('abento_parent_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are special associations for the [CDS composition tree](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
