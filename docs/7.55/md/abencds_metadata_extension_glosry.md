CDS Metadata Extension

A [CDS object](javascript:call_link\('abencds_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") defined in a piece of [DDLX source code](javascript:call_link\('abenddlx_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with [language elements](javascript:call_link\('abencds_f1_ddlx_syntax.htm'\)) of the [CDS DDL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In a CDS metadata extension (MDE), [CDS annotations](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are specified for a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") outside its [data definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A CDS metadata extension is always assigned to a layer such as industry, partner or customer and can be linked to a [CDS variant](javascript:call_link\('abencds_variant_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to control its [evaluation](javascript:call_link\('abencds_meta_data_extension_eval.htm'\)).
