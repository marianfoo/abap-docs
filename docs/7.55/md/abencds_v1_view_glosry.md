CDS DDIC-based view

[CDS view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is technically based on a [CDS-managed DDIC view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_mngdddic_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A CDS DDIC-based view is defined with the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") statement [DEFINE VIEW ddic\_based\_view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_define_view_v1.htm).
