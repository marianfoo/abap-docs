character literal

Also referred to as a text literal. [Literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenliteral_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with [character-like data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencharlike_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and whose value is a [character string](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencharacter_string_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In general ABAP, character literals are [untyped literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), character literals can also be [typed literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
