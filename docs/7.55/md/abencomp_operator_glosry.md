comparison operator

[Relational operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrel_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that links two or more [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [comparison expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencomparison_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of an [SQL condition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensql_cond_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
