composition source

The [association source](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), that is the data source that serves as the source of the composition.
