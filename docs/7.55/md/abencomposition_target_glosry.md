composition target

The [association target](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenassociation_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_composition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), that is the data source that serves as the target of the composition.
