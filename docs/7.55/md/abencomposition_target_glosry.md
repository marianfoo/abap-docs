composition target

The [association target](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), that is the data source that serves as the target of the composition.
