concatenation

A concatenation merges the character-like or byte-like content of different operands into one result. Concatenations can be made using the [concatenation operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenconcatenation_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the [concatenation assignment operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenconcat_assignment_op_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [concatenation functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenconcatenation_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or the [CONCATENATE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapconcatenate.htm) statement.
