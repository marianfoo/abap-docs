[Named data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abennamed_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") whose value cannot be changed during the runtime of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
