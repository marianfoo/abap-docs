Coverage Analyzer

Tool for recording how often programs or individual [processing blocks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are executed, classified by time period, [user](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenuser_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or subject area. It is called using [transaction code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentransaction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") SCOV.
