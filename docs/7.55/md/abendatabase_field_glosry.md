database field

Smallest logical unit of the [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [database table](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The properties of a database field of a [DDIC database table](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are defined by a [data element](javascript:call_link\('abendata_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
