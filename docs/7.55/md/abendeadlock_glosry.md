deadlock

Blocked access to commonly files (such as of a [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) due to an incorrect locking concept (preventing competing access) or an incorrect application of a locking concept.
