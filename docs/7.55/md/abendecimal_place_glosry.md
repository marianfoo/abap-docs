decimal place

Also referred to as a fractional digit. Decimal places contain the fractional part of a decimal number and are noted to the right of the decimal separator, regardless of the number system. In the case of [decimal numbers](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendecimal_number_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the decimal places are the [places](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenplace_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that come after the [decimal separator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
