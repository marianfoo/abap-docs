declaration

Defines the properties of local program entities. [Data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [interfaces](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [field symbols](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenfield_symbol_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are declared. Declarations are made either with [declaration statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendeclaration_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or as [inline declarations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninline_declaration_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using [declaration expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendeclaration_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The declared unit is visible statically as soon as it is declared and is valid throughout the entire [context](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenobj_context_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
