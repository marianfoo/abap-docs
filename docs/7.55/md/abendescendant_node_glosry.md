descendant node

The descendant nodes of a [parent node](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparent_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenhierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are its [child nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenchild_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), their child nodes, and so on.
