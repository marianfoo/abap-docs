details list

[List](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [list level](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_level_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") greater than 0. List outputs in [event blocks](javascript:call_link\('abenevent_block_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the [interactive list processing](javascript:call_link\('abeninteractive_list_proces_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") writes to details lists.
