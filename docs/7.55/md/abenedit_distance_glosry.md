edit distance

Also referred to as the [Levenshtein distance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenlevenshtein_distance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Minimum number of insert, delete, and replace operations required to transform one string to another.
