Enhancement Builder

Tool in the [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for creation and administration of [enhancements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenenhancement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") within the [enhancement concept](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenenhancement_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
