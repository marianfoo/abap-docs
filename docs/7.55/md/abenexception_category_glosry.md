exception category

Classification of [class-based exceptions](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_based_exception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") correspondent to the [superclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensuperclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of their [exception classes](javascript:call_link\('abenexception_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The exception category determines how the exceptions must be declared in the [procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenprocedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
