exception

Error situation during execution of an [ABAP program](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Exceptions are [catchable](javascript:call_link\('abencatchable_exception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (statements [TRY](javascript:call_link\('abaptry.htm'\)), [CATCH](javascript:call_link\('abapcatch_try.htm'\))) or [uncatchable](javascript:call_link\('abenuncatchable_exception_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Uncatchable exceptions result in a [runtime error](javascript:call_link\('abenruntime_error_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
