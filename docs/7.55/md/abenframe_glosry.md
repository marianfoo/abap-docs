frame

[Screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used to group other screen elements and on which no [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are possible. A frame is not linked to a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
