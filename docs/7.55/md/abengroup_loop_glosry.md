group loop

Loop across the groups of an [internal table](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") formed by the addition [GROUP BY](javascript:call_link\('abaploop_at_itab_group_by.htm'\)) of the statement [LOOP AT](javascript:call_link\('abaploop_at_itab_variants.htm'\)). A group loop can contain [member loops](javascript:call_link\('abenmember_loop_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for the evaluation of the groups.
