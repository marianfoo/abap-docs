hierarchy

Arrangement of objects in hierarchical structures. The objects of a hierarchy are [hierarchy nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are linked to each other via [hierarchy edges](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenhierarchy_edge_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
