identity transformation

[XSL transformation](javascript:call_link\('abenxsl_transformation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") called ID delivered by SAP for reading or writing the canonical formats [asXML](javascript:call_link\('abenasxml_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [asJSON](javascript:call_link\('abenasjson_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Calls of this transformation are handled in special ways internally, depending on the formats involved.
