internal procedure call

Call of a [processing block](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ([procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) of the same program. The required program does not have to be reloaded into the [internal session](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninternal_session_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
