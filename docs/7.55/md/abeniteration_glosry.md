iteration

Repeated processing of a [statement block](javascript:call_link\('abenstatement_block_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [loop](javascript:call_link\('abenloop_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or an [expression](javascript:call_link\('abenexpression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with an [iteration expression](javascript:call_link\('abeniteration_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
