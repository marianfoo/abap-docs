join condition

[SQL condition](javascript:call_link\('abensql_cond_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [CDS DDL condition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_conditional_expression_v2.htm'\)) specified after the addition ON of a [join expression](javascript:call_link\('abenjoin_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Also called ON condition.
