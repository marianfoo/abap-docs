join expression

Implementation of a [join](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenjoin_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using the addition JOIN. Join expressions are possible in [Queries](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenquery_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined by the [SELECT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapselect.htm) statement of [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the SELECT statement of [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") ([SELECT in CDS view entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_select_statement_v2.htm),[SELECT in CDS DDIC-based views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_select_statement_v2.htm))
