local data

Local data is declared in [procedures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and is only known there. See also [global data](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprg_glbl_data_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
