lock dependent

Defines the property of entities that depend on the lock status of a [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [root entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenroot_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
