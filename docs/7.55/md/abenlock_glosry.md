lock

Locks control the access to data by multiple users. Regarding access to [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), there are [shared locks](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [exclusive locks](javascript:call_link\('abenexclusive_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") implemented as [database locks](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [SAP locks](javascript:call_link\('abensap_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Regarding access to [shared objects](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_objects_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), there are so-called [area locks](javascript:call_link\('abenarea_lock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
