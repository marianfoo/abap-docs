logical operator

[Operator](javascript:call_link\('abenoperator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [logical expression](javascript:call_link\('abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Either a [Boolean operator](javascript:call_link\('abenboolean_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for the association or negation of logical expressions or a [relational operator](javascript:call_link\('abenrel_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for [relational expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
