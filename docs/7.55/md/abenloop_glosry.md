loop

[Control structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencontrol_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that performs an [iteration](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeniteration_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Consists of a [statement block](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenstatement_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined by the keywords ([DO - ENDDO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapdo.htm), [WHILE - ENDWHILE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapwhile.htm), [LOOP - ENDLOOP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abaploop_at_itab.htm), [PROVIDE - ENDPROVIDE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapprovide.htm), [SELECT - ENDSELECT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapselect.htm)) and that can be executed several times.
