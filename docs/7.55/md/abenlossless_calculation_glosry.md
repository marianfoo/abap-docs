lossless calculation

[Arithmetic](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenarithmetic_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") calculation where no rounding occurs. The [lossless operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenlossless_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [EXACT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_exact.htm) or the addition EXACT of the obsolete statement [COMPUTE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapcompute.htm) can be used to check whether a calculation is lossless.
