main annotation

Also referred to as root annotation. The [CDS annotation](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") defined after the statement [DEFINE ANNOTATION](javascript:call_link\('abencds_f1_define_annotation.htm'\)) in a [CDS annotation definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or a name of an [annotation](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") specified on its own or in front of a period in the [annotation syntax](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_syntax_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A main annotation can be structured using [subannotations](javascript:call_link\('abensub_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
