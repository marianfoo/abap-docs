mathematical notation

Representation of a number as a digit sequence with a possible [decimal separator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where the plus/minus sign is placed in front of the digits.
