menu entry

Textual [control element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencontrol_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [menu](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenmenu_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is linked with a [function code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenfunction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and can be selected simply by clicking it.
