message maintenance

Tool in the [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for creating and maintaining [messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenmessage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
