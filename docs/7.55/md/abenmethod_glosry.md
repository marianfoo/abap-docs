method

[Procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is declared as a [component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of an [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The two possible methods are [instance methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninstance_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [static methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenstatic_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A method can be implemented only in the [implementation part](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenimplementation_part_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of its class, between [METHOD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapmethod.htm) and [ENDMETHOD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapendmethod.htm).
