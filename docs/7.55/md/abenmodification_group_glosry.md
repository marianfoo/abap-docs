modification group

Grouping of multiple [screen elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") under a three-digit abbreviation in order to commonly modify its display properties with the statement [MODIFY SCREEN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapmodify_screen.htm). Screen elements are assigned to modification groups in the [Screen Painter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_painter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or in the [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using the addition [MODIF ID](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapselection-screen_modif_id.htm) for [selection screens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenselection_screen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A screen element can be assigned to up to four modification groups. The structure [SCREEN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen.htm) contains four components for the identification of modification groups.
