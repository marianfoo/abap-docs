Modification 1

Support for the New 12 Hour Format

The new addition [ENVIRONMENT TIME FORMAT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapwrite_to_options.htm) to the WRITE TO and WRITE statements supports the new [12 hour format](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abennews-710-others.htm).

The [predefined time formats](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenwrite_formats.htm) of the statements WRITE TO and WRITE cannot be influenced automatically due to downward compatibility.

Hint

This modification was also added to Release 7.0 in SP14.
