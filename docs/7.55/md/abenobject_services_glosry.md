Object Services

OS for short. [Interfaces](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") from the [class library](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_library_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the prefixes IF\_OS\_ and CL\_OS\_. Object services provide [persistence services](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenpersistence_service_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [transaction services](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentransaction_service_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are used to manage [persistent objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenpersistent_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and object-oriented transactions. There is also a [query service](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenquery_service_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for finding and loading persistent objects.
