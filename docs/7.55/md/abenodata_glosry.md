OData

Short for Open Data Protocol. OData is a standardized protocol for defining and consuming [RESTful](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrestful_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") APIs. For [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [SAP Gateway](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensap_gateway_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") provides such APIs.
