operand

Component of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of a [CDS data definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Operands can be linked via [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to form [expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that in turn can be listed at certain [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

There are different types of operands:

-   [ABAP operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_operand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")

-   [CDS operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_operand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")
