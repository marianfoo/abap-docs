operand position

Position of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of a [CDS data definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") at which an [operand](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is listed. There are [reader](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenreading_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [write positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenwriting_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). An operand position is typed with an [operand type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperand_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [Data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperands_data_objects.htm) can usually be listed at operand positions. [Functions and expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoperands_expressions.htm) are also possible at certain operand positions.
