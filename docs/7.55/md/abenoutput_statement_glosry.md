output statement

Statement for writing into a [list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenlist_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Output statements are [WRITE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapwrite-.htm) and [ULINE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapuline.htm). There are also statements for positioning the [list cursor](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenlist_cursor_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
