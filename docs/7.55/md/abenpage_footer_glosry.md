page footer

Reserved lines at the bottom of a page in a [list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenlist_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The page footer is defined in the [program-initiating statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprogram_init_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and can be filled during the [event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenevent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [END-OF-PAGE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapend-of-page.htm).
