PAI

Short for Process After Input. [Dynpro event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") raised by a [user action](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abengui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). During PAI time, the contents of [dynpro fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are transferred to [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the same name in the [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
