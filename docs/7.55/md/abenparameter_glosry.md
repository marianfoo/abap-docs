parameter

A component of a [selection screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenselection_screen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (defined by [PARAMETERS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapparameters.htm)) which is displayed as an [input field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninput_output_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and which is represented internally as an [elementary data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenelementary_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A parameter is a also a Component of the [parameter interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparameter_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
