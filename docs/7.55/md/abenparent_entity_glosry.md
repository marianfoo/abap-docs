parent entity

A [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition tree](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or the tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that uses a [CDS composition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to define a [child entity](javascript:call_link\('abenchild_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is its parent entity. A parent entity can have multiple child entities. Each child entity must expose a [to-parent association](javascript:call_link\('abento_parent_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with its parent entity.
