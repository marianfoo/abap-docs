parent entity

A [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or the tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that uses a [CDS composition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_composition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to define a [child entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is its parent entity. A parent entity can have multiple child entities. Each child entity must expose a [to-parent association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abento_parent_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with its parent entity.
