pass by category

Method of transferring data from [actual parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenactual_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to [formal parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Either [pass by reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenpass_by_reference_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [pass by value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenpass_by_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The pass by category is specified in the definition of a formal parameter.
