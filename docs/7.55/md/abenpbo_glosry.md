PBO

Short for Process Before Output. [Dynpro event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") raised by the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") before a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is sent to [SAP GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensap_gui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). After the PBO is processed, [dynpro fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") receive the contents of [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the same name as the [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
