POSIX regular expression

[Regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenregular_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is expressed in [POSIX syntax](javascript:call_link\('abenregex_posix_syntax.htm'\)) and processed by the [Boost.Regex Library](http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_31_0/libs/regex/doc/index.html) implemented in the [ABAP Kernel](javascript:call_link\('abenkernel_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See also [PCRE regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenpcre_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
