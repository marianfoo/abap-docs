predicate method

[Functional method](javascript:call_link\('abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") whose [return value](javascript:call_link\('abenreturn_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") has the type abap\_bool from the [type pool](javascript:call_link\('abentype_pool_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ABAP. A predicate method is particularly well suited for [predicative method calls](javascript:call_link\('abenpredicative_method_call_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), but is not a prerequisite.
