procedure

[Processing block](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can be called using an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and that is returned after the call behind call position in the ABAP Program. A procedure has a [parameter interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparameter_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and a local [data area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Possible procedures are [methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenmethod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [function modules](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenfunction_module_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [subroutines](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubroutine_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") .
