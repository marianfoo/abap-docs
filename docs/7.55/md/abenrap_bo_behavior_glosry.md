RAP Business Object Behavior

RAP BO Behavior for short. The behavior of a [RAP Business Object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is defined in a [CDS Behavior Definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and implemented in an [ABAP behavior implementation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbehavior_implement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
