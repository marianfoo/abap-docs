RAP Business Object Behavior

RAP BO Behavior for short. The behavior of a [RAP Business Object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is defined in a [CDS Behavior Definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and implemented in an [ABAP behavior implementation](javascript:call_link\('abenbehavior_implement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
