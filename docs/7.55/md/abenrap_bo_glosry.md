RAP Business Object

RAP BO for short. [Business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbusiness_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the context of [ABAP RAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_rap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A RAP BO consists mainly of [CDS entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [CDS behavior definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [ABAP behavior implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbehavior_implement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A RAP BO can be exposed as a [business service](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenbusiness_service_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for access outside [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and is handled by the [RAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_runtime_framework_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") then. For access to RAP BOs from inside AS ABAP, ABAP contains a subset called [ABAP Entity Manipulation Language (EML)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenaeml_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
