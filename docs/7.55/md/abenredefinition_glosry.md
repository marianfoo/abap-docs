redefinition

Term in [ABAP Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_objects_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). For a redefinition, an [instance method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninstance_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [subclass](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be reimplemented without changing the [parameter interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparameter_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
