relational operator

Superordinate term for [comparison operators](javascript:call_link\('abencomp_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [predicate operators](javascript:call_link\('abenpredicate_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators occur in [relational expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Relational operators belong to the [logical operators](javascript:call_link\('abenlogical_operator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
