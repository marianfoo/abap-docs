root entity

The top [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of the tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The addition ROOT must be specified in the definition of a root entity with the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
