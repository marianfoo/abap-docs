root entity

The top [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition tree](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or of the tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The addition ROOT must be specified in the definition of a root entity with the [CDS DDL](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
