SADL

Short for Service Adaptation Definition Language. Infrastructure for model-based reading and processing of data. The [RAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_runtime_framework_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") uses SADL internally for its orchestration framework that controls the runtime behavior of [RAP business objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"),
