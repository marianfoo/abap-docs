selection text

Part of the [text elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentext_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A selection text or a text from the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be assigned to every [parameter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [selection criterion](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenselection_criterion_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined using [PARAMETERS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapparameters.htm) or [SELECT-OPTIONS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abapselect-options.htm). It is then displayed for the corresponding [input fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninput_output_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [selection screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenselection_screen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
