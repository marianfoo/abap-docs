single inheritance

[Inheritance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninheritance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") method where, unlike multiple inheritance, a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can have multiple [subclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") but only one direct [superclass](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensuperclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [inheritance hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninheritance_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
