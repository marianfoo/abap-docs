splitter control

Splitter controls enable the display of two [subscreens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") next to each other or above one another. A splitter control is a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that divides the area that it covers into two [subscreen areas](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubscreen_arrea_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The boundary between the areas can be moved using a [sash](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensash_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
