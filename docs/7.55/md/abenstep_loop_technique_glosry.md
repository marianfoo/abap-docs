step loop technique

Technique in [dynpro flow logic](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_flow_logic_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for processing tabular [screen elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The step loop technique is used to process [table controls](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentable_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and their predecessors, namely the [step loops](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenstep_loop_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
