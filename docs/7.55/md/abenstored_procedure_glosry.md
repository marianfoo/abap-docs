stored procedure

[Database procedure](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_procedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Procedural [database object](javascript:call_link\('abendb_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that can be implemented and called depending on the platform. A stored procedure generally combines sequences of multiple [SQL](javascript:call_link\('abensql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") statements and contains additional instructions for controlling the sequence. [ABAP-managed](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_managed_db_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") stored procedures are [AMDP procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenamdp_procedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [AMDP functions](javascript:call_link\('abenamdp_function_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
