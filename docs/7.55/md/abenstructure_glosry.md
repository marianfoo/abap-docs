structure

[Data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with a [structured type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenstructured_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that consists of components.. In [ABAP programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), whole structures or just components of structures can be accessed. Structures that contain only [character-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [flat](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenflat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can also be handled like [elementary data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenelementary_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
