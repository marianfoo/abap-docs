subscreen area

Area within a [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which other screens can be displayed as [subscreens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
