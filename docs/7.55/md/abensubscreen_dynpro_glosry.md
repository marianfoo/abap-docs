subscreen dynpro

[Dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") whose [screen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be displayed either directly as a [subscreen](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [subscreen area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensubscreen_arrea_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or in a [tabstrip](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentabstrip_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [splitter control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensplitter_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
