substructure

Structured [component](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [nested structure](javascript:call_link\('abennested_structure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A substructure can be declared as a [boxed component](javascript:call_link\('abenboxed_component_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
