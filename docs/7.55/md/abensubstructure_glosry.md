substructure

Structured [component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [nested structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abennested_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A substructure can be declared as a [boxed component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenboxed_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
