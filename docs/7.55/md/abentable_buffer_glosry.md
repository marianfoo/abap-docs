table buffer

Area in the [shared memory](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenshared_memory_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [table buffering](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abentable_buffering_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The table buffer is managed by the [database interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abendatabase_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
