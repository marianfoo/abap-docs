CDS to-parent association

Special [CDS association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is a prerequisite for defining a [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as the [child entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [CDS composition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abencds_composition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The ON conditions of a to-parent association are implicitly transferred to the CDS composition of the parent entity too. A CDS entity can define and expose exactly one to-parent association.
