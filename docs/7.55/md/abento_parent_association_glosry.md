CDS to-parent association

Special [CDS association](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is a prerequisite for defining a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as the [child entity](javascript:call_link\('abenchild_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [parent entity](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [CDS composition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The ON conditions of a to-parent association are implicitly transferred to the CDS composition of the parent entity too. A CDS entity can define and expose exactly one to-parent association.
