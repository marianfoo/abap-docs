uncatchable exception

[Exception](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenexception_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not assigned to an [exception class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenexception_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and that cannot be handled in the program by [TRY - CATCH - ENDTRY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abaptry.htm). Uncatchable exceptions are raised by the [ABAP runtime environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_runtime_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and are usually a response to error situations that cannot be handled properly in the program. An uncatchable exception always produces a [runtime error](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenruntime_error_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
