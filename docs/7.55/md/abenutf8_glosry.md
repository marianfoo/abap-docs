UTF-8

8-bit [Unicode character representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenunicode_char_represent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [ASCII](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenascii_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") characters are represented by a single byte. Other European characters are represented by two bytes. Most Asian characters are represented by three bytes. The characters in the [surrogate area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensurrogate_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are addressed directly using four bytes.
