UTF-8

8-bit [Unicode character representation](javascript:call_link\('abenunicode_char_represent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [ASCII](javascript:call_link\('abenascii_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") characters are represented by a single byte. Other European characters are represented by two bytes. Most Asian characters are represented by three bytes. The characters in the [surrogate area](javascript:call_link\('abensurrogate_area_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are addressed directly using four bytes.
