writer stream

Also referred to as [output stream](javascript:call_link\('abenoutput_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Either a [data stream](javascript:call_link\('abendata_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is connected to a [streaming data sink](javascript:call_link\('abenstreaming_data_sink_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [filter stream](javascript:call_link\('abenfilter_stream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that writes data to another stream. The direction of a writer stream always goes toward the data sink. In a writer stream data can only be written.
