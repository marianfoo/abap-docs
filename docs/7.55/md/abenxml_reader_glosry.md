XML reader

[Instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abeninstance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [sXML Library](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abensxml_library_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that implements the [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") IF\_SXML\_READER. An XML reader enables the parsing of [XML](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenxml_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") data in different [formats](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_755_index_htm/7.55/en-US/abenabap_sxml_lib_formats.htm).
