FREE - Short Reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapfree_dataobject.htm)

Syntax

FREE dobj.

Effect

Initializes a data object dobj by assigning the type-dependent [initial value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abeninitial_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In the case of internal tables, the entire memory area occupied by lines is freed.
