TABLES - Short Reference

[Reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abaptables.htm)

Syntax

TABLES table\_wa.

Effect

Declares an [interface work area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abeninterface_work_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") table\_wa for data transfer to and from [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and from [logical databases](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogical_data_base_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
