ABAP operand

Syntax element of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") at an [operand position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Specified as a [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [formal parameter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [field symbol](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenfield_symbol_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [dereferenced](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendereferencing_operat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [data reference](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendata_reference_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [built-in function](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenbuiltin_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [functional method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or also by listing it directly. Operands can be combined with [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to form [expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that in turn can be listed at certain [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
