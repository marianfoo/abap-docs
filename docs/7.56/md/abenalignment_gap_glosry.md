alignment gap

Bytes within a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are inserted either before [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with alignment requirements or before the end of the structure to provide the necessary [alignment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenalignment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Alignment gaps contribute to the length of a structure. The binary content of alignment gaps is not defined and must not be evaluated.
