AMDP procedure

[Database procedure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendatabase_procedure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") implemented in an [AMDP procedure implementation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenamdp_procedure_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [AMDP class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenamdp_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and replicated from there to the [database system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendatabase_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [AMDP function](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenamdp_class_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
