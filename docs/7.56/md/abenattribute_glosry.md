attribute

[Data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") declared within a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or an [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
