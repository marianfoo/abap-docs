authorization field

Smallest unit of an [authorization object](javascript:call_link\('abenauthorization_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). An authorization field represents either data such as a [key field](javascript:call_link\('abenkey_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [DDIC database table](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or activities such as reading or changing. Activities are specified as abbreviations that are stored in the DDIC database table TACT and in TACTZ for customers.
