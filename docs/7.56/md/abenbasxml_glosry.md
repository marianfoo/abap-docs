basXML

Binary ABAP Serialization XML. Representation of [asXML](javascript:call_link\('abenasxml_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [Binary XML](javascript:call_link\('abenbinary_xml_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). basXML is used, among other things, as an [RFC protocol](javascript:call_link\('abenrfc_protocol.htm'\)) with the same name.
