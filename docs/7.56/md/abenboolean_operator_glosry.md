Boolean operator

Negates or combines [logical expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The possible Boolean operators are [NOT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogexp_not.htm), [AND](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogexp_and.htm), [OR](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogexp_or.htm), and [EQUIV](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogexp_equiv.htm). Boolean operators belong to the [logical operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlogical_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
