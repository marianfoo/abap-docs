Business Function

BF for short. [Repository object](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that represents a self-contained (business) function within the scope of the [Switch Framework](javascript:call_link\('abenswitch_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Multiple business functions are grouped into a [Business Function Set](javascript:call_link\('abenbusiness_function_set_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Technically, a business function is a grouping of [switches](javascript:call_link\('abenswitch_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that serve the same function. When a business function is activated, the corresponding switches are activated as well. Business functions are created in [transaction](javascript:call_link\('abentransaction_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") SFW2 and switched in transaction SFW5.
