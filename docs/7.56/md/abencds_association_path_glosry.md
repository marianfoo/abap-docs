CDS association path

Sequence of [CDS associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that link [CDS entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with [association targets](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenassociation_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). CDS association paths can be specified in the form of [SQL path expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensql_path_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
