CDS calculated quantity

Component of a [CDS view entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_v2_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A calculated quantity is always the result of a calculation using [CDS amount](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_amount_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and / or [CDS quantity fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_quantity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The DDIC data type is DECFLOAT34. An ABAP CDS calculated quantity must be linked with a [CDS calculated unit](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_calculated_unit_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the same CDS view entity that specifies a self-defined unit for the calculated quantity.
