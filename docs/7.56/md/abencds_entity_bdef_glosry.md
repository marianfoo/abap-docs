entity behavior definition

Component of a [CDS behavior definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (BDEF). A BDEF consists of a [header part](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_bdef_header_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and at least one entity behavior definition, referring to a [business object's](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenbusiness_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [root entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenroot_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Further entity behavior definitions referring to [child entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are optional.
