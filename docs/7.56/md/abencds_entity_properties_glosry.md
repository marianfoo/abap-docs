entity behavior characteristics

The entity behavior characteristics are that part of an [entity behavior definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_bdef_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that stand after define behavior for and before the curly brackets. The entity behavior characteristics define additional properties of a behavior definition on entity level.
