entity behavior characteristics

The entity behavior characteristics are that part of an [entity behavior definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_entity_bdef_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that stand after define behavior for and before the curly brackets. The entity behavior characteristics define additional properties of a behavior definition on entity level.
