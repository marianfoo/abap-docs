CDS literal

Elementary CDS operand defined by direct character-like specification of a value in a [CDS object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In general [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), untyped [numeric literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abennumeric_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and untyped [character literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharacter_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are available. In [CDS view entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_v2_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [CDS hierarchies](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), both [typed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [untyped](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") literals are available.

See also: [literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenliteral_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
