CDS object

[Repository object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). CDS objects are usually defined and transported in [CDS source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The object itself is defined by the activation of its source code-based definition. Possible CDS objects are [CDS entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [CDS annotations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [CDS metadata extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_metadata_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [CDS variants](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_variant_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [CDS RAP objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_rap_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [CDS roles](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_role_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
