CDS projected entity

Used in the context of [CDS projection views](javascript:call_link\('abencds_projection_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The [CDS view](javascript:call_link\('abencds_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is used as basis of a CDS projection view, and that is defined after the keyword AS PROJECTION ON is called projected entity.
