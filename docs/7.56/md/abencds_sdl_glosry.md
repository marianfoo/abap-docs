CDS SDL

Service Definition Language of [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). CDS SDL contains language elements for [CDS service definitions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_service_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [SDL source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensdl_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
