character-like expression position

[Character-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharlike_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [read position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenread_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which, in addition to a [character-like data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [character-like expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharlike_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can also be specified.
