checkpoint

Superordinate term for [assertions](javascript:call_link\('abenassertion_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [breakpoints](javascript:call_link\('abenbreakpoint_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or [logpoints](javascript:call_link\('abenlogpoint_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The statements [ASSERT](javascript:call_link\('abapassert.htm'\)), [BREAK-POINT](javascript:call_link\('abapbreak-point.htm'\)), and [LOG-POINT](javascript:call_link\('abaplog-point.htm'\)) for defining checkpoints are not operative but equip the program for test purposes. Inactive checkpoints are ignored during program execution.
