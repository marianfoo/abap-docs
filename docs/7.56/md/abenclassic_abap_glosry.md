classic ABAP

ABAP development in a [classic ABAP development environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenclassic_abap_dev_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Compared to [strict ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstrict_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), there are no restrictions regarding [ABAP language versions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_version_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), access to [released APIs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenreleased_api_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or usage of tools.
