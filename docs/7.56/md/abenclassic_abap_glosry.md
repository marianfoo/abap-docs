classic ABAP

ABAP development in a [classic ABAP development environment](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_abap_dev_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Compared to [strict ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenstrict_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), there are no restrictions regarding [ABAP language versions](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_version_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), access to [released APIs](javascript:call_link\('abenreleased_api_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or usage of tools.
