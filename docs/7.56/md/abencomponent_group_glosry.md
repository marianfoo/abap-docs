component group

Also [named include](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abennamed_include_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Group of [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are included from another structure under one name. The components can be addressed by this name.
