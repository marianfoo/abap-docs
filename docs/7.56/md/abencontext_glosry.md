context

Contexts are obsolete [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are composed of [key fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenkey_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to be entered, relationships between these fields, and further fields that can be derived from them. They are defined [abstractly](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabstract_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [Context Builder](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencontext_builder_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and used during runtime as temporary objects. Contexts can be processed with the statements [CONTEXTS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapcontexts.htm), [DEMAND](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapdemand.htm), and [SUPPLY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapsupply.htm).
