currency field

A currency field is linked to a [currency key](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencurrency_key_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and it is used for entering an amount in a particular currency. Currency fields are available in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (DDIC) and in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), but the possible data types and handling differs.
