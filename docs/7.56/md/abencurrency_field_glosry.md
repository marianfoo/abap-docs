currency field

A currency field is linked to a [currency key](javascript:call_link\('abencurrency_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and it is used for entering an amount in a particular currency. Currency fields are available in [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (DDIC) and in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), but the possible data types and handling differs.
