custom control

Area on a [screen layout](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which [GUI controls](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abengui_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be displayed.
