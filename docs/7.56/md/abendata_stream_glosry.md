data stream

[Stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is either directly connected to a [streaming data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstreaming_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a [reader stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenreader_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or as a [writer stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenwriter_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") directly with a [streaming data sink](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstreaming_data_sink_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
