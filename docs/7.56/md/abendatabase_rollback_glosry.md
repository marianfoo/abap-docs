database rollback

End of a [database LUW](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in which all modifying database operations are undone until the beginning of the LUW. In an [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenas_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), database rollbacks are triggered implicitly, as well as by explicit requests.
