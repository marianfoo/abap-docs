descendant node

The descendant nodes of a [parent node](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are its [child nodes](javascript:call_link\('abenchild_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), their child nodes, and so on.
