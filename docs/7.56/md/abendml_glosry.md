DML

Short for Data Manipulation Language. Subset of [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The statements in DML perform [CRUD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencrud_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") operations on [relational](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrelational_database_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In ABAP, DML is mapped by [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
