elementary data type

[Data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of fixed or variable length that is neither [structured](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstructured_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), nor a [table type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abentable_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [reference type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenreference_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In particular, the [built-in ABAP types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenbuiltin_abap_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are elementary.
