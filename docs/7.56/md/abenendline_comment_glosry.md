end of line comment

Section of a program line, following an [ABAP statement](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or part of a statement, that is identified as a [comment](javascript:call_link\('abencomment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") by a double quotation mark (").
