Enhancement Builder

Tool in the [ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for creating and administering [enhancements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenenhancement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") within the [enhancement concept](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenenhancement_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
