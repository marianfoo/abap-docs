enhancement implementation

A [repository object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used for the administration of [enhancements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenenhancement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of other repository objects. Enhancement implementations are components of a hierarchical tree structure of which the branches represent [composite](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencomp_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") enhancement implementations, and the leaves are [simple enhancement implementations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensimple_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
