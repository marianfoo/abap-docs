[Message](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenmessage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [message type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenmessage_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") E.
