functional method call

Call of a [functional method](javascript:call_link\('abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a suitable [read position](javascript:call_link\('abenread_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), where the [return value](javascript:call_link\('abenreturn_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the method is used as an [ABAP operand](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_operand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
