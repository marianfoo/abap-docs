inheritance hierarchy

Hierarchical relationship between [superclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensuperclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [subclasses](javascript:call_link\('abensubclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [inheritance](javascript:call_link\('abeninheritance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") functions. When [single inheritance](javascript:call_link\('abensingle_inheritance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is used, the inheritance hierarchy forms an [inheritance tree](javascript:call_link\('abeninheritance_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
