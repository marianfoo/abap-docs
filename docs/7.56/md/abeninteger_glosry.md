integer

[Numeric data type](javascript:call_link\('abennumeric_data_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for storing [integer numbers](javascript:call_link\('abeninteger_number_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of different value ranges. The associated [built-in ABAP types](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_abap_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are ([b](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_types_numeric.htm'\)), [s](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_types_numeric.htm'\))), [i](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_types_numeric.htm'\)), and [int8](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_types_numeric.htm'\)). The internal representation takes place in the integer representation of the current platform.
