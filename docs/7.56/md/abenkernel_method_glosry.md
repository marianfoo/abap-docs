kernel method

[Method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenmethod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is implemented in the [kernel](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenkernel_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") instead of in an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
