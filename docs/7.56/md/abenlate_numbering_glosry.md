late numbering

A concept where newly created entity instances are given their final key only shortly before saving in the database. Until then, the business logic uses a temporary key that has to be replaced when saving the data of the [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
