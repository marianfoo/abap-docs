LET expression

A LET expression is a subexpression of a [constructor expression](javascript:call_link\('abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that declares [variables](javascript:call_link\('abenvariable_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [field symbols](javascript:call_link\('abenfield_symbol_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as local helper fields.
