literal condition

Part of an [access condition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenaccess_condition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [CDS role](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_role_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that associates an element of a [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with a literal value.
