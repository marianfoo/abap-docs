locale

Part of the [text environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abentext_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Defines the language-specific and country-specific characteristics of characters, such as the sort sequence or the uppercase/lowercase conversion. Local properties are defined in a library independent of the operating system.
