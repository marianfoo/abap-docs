lock

Locks control the access to data by multiple users. Regarding access to [database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), there are [shared locks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenshared_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [exclusive locks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenexclusive_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") implemented as [database locks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendatabase_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [SAP locks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensap_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Regarding access to [shared objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenshared_objects_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), there are [area locks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenarea_lock_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
