LUW

Short for Logical unit of work. Time between two consistent states of the [database](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See also [SAP LUW](javascript:call_link\('abensap_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [database LUW](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
