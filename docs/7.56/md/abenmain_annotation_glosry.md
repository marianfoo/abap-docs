main annotation

Also referred to as root annotation. The [CDS annotation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined after the statement [DEFINE ANNOTATION](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_f1_define_annotation.htm) in a [CDS annotation definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or a name of an [annotation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") specified on its own or in front of a period in the [annotation syntax](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencds_annotation_syntax_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A main annotation can be structured using [subannotations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensub_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
