Menu Painter

[ABAP Workbench](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") tool for creating the [GUI status](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abengui_status_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and its components.
