multiple selection

[Selection criterium](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenselection_criterion_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for which the [selection table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenselection_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is filled with more than one line.
