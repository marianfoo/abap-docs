namespace prefix

Name of a [prefix namespace](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenprefix_name_space_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with five to ten characters that must contain a forward slash character (/.../) in the first and final place. The names of the [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a prefix namespace are introduced by the associated namespace prefix.
