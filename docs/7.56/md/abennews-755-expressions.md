Expressions and Functions in Release 7.55

Modification

Calculation assignments in constructor operator REDUCE

In the assignments behind the addition NEXT of the constructor operator [REDUCE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_reduce.htm) the [calculation assignment operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencalc_assignment_op_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") +=, +=, \*=, /= or &&=, can be used now and the respective rules apply.
