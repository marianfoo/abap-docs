page header

Lines at the start of a page in a [list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenclassic_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are excluded from vertical (top to bottom) scrolling. Can be filled with the content of the [standard page header](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstandard_page_header_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and during the [event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenevent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [TOP-OF-PAGE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abaptop-of-page.htm).
