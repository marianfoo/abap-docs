PCRE regular expression

[Perl Compatible Regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenpcre_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is expressed in [PCRE syntax](javascript:call_link\('abenregex_pcre_syntax.htm'\)) and processed by the [PCRE2 Library](https://www.pcre.org/) implemented in the [ABAP Kernel](javascript:call_link\('abenkernel_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or by the [PCRE1 Library](https://www.pcre.org/) of the [SAP HANA Database](javascript:call_link\('abenhana_database_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See also [XPath regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenxpath_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [XSD regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenxsd_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [POSIX regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenposix_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (obsolete).
