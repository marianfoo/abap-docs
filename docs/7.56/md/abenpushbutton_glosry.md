pushbutton

[Control element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencontrol_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abengui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is connected to a [function code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenfunction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and which can be selected by clicking on it. In [SAP GUI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensap_gui_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), pushbuttons can be defined both as [screen elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and in [standard toolbars](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstandard_toolbar_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [application toolbars](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenapplication_toolbar_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
