Rabax

Short for Runtime exit. Rabax is an internal SAP term describing a program crash after a [runtime error](javascript:call_link\('abenruntime_error_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The corresponding [ABAP kernel](javascript:call_link\('abenkernel_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") module, also called Rabax, performs the tasks required to complete the [internal session](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_session_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and creates the associated [short dump](javascript:call_link\('abenshort_dump_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
