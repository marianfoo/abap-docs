repository browser

Standard layout of the [Object Navigator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenobject_navigator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for the structured display of [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") sorted according to their type.
