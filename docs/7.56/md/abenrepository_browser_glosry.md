repository browser

Standard layout of the [Object Navigator](javascript:call_link\('abenobject_navigator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for the structured display of [repository objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") sorted according to their type.
