S/4HANA Cloud ABAP environment

[Strict ABAP development environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstrict_abap_dev_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (aka [embedded Steampunk](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenembedded_steampunk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) for customer developments in [ABAP platform cloud](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_platform_cloud_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Such developments are governed by the restricted rules of the [SAP BTP ABAP environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensap_btp_abap_env_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (aka [Steampunk](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensteampunk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")).
