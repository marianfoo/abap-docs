SAP spool system

Process of an [AS instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenas_instance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for managing sequential data streams that are stored as [spool lists](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenspool_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), printed on a printer, or stored using [ArchiveLink](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenarchivelink_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [ABAP programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can create [spool requests](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenspool_request_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") during the creation of lists by including pages of lists as parts of a [spool list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenspool_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") output.
