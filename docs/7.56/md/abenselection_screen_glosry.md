selection screen

Special [dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can be defined without the use of the [Screen Painter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenscreen_painter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by using the statements [SELECTION-SCREEN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapselection-screen.htm), [PARAMETERS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapparameters.htm), or [SELECT-OPTIONS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abapselect-options.htm) in the [global declaration part](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenglobal_declaration_sect_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [executable programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenexecutable_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [function pools](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenfunction_pool_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [module pools](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenmodul_pool_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Selection screens are processed by the [ABAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), which raises [selection screen events](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenselection_screen_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). There are [standard selection screens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstandard_selscreen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [standalone selection screens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenstand-alone_sel_screen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
