Standard ABAP

[Unrestricted ABAP language version](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenunrestricted_version_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with unrestricted language scope and unrestricted access to [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). For Standard ABAP, the [Unicode checks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenunicode_check_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are effective that are required in an [Unicode systems](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenunicode_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
