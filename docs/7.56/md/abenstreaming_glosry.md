streaming

The sequential and segmental transfer of data or the transfer of [streams](javascript:call_link\('abenstream_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Streaming is intended for all possible resources and is currently implemented for [LOBs](javascript:call_link\('abenlob_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in ABAP SQL, [strings](javascript:call_link\('abenstring_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and [internal tables](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
