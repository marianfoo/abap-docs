tab page

Also referred to as tab. Page within a [tabstrip control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abentabstrip_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Each tab page has a [tab title](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abentab_title_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
