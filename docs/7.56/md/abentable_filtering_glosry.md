table filtering

Creates the content of an [internal table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by filtering the lines of an internal table in a [constructor expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the [filter operator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenfilter_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [FILTER](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_filter.htm).
