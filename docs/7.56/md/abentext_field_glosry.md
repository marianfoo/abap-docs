text field

In ABAP, [character-like data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharlike_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for saving [character strings](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencharacter_string_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of fixed length or a similar [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The corresponding [built-in ABAP type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenbuiltin_abap_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is [c](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenbuiltin_types_character.htm). The internal representation takes place in the [character representation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenchar_representation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the current [code page](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abencodepage_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

In [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is used purely for displaying text and on which no [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are possible. A text field of a dynpro is not linked to a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
