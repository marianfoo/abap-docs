update session

[User session](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenuser_session_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for [synchronous](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensynchronous_update_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [asynchronous updates](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenasynchronous_update_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). [Local updates](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenlocal_update_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are not carried out in a separate update session.
