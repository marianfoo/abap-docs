SAP HANA XSC

Short for SAP HANA Extended Application Services Classic Model. Previous version of [XS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenxs_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and predecessor to [XSA](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenxsa_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). XSC objects are stored in the [SAP HANA repository](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and are usually developed and managed in [SAP HANA Studio](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abenhana_studio_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). SAP HANA XSC is not supported by a [SAP HANA Cloud database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_756_index_htm/7.56/en-US/abensap_hana_cloud_db_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
