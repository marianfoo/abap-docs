SAP HANA XSC

Short for SAP HANA Extended Application Services Classic Model. Previous version of [XS](javascript:call_link\('abenxs_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and predecessor to [XSA](javascript:call_link\('abenxsa_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). XSC objects are stored in the [SAP HANA repository](javascript:call_link\('abensap_hana_repository_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and are usually developed and managed in [SAP HANA Studio](javascript:call_link\('abenhana_studio_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). SAP HANA XSC is not supported by a [SAP HANA Cloud database](javascript:call_link\('abensap_hana_cloud_db_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
