XSD regular expression

[Regular expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenregular_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") using the syntax and semantics described under [Regular expressions in XSD 1.0 and 1.1](https://www.w3.org/XML/2008/03/xsdl-regex/re.xml). ABAP supports [XSD syntax](javascript:call_link\('abenregex_xsd_syntax.htm'\)) for regular expressions by transforming them to [PCRE](javascript:call_link\('abenpcre_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") internally. See also [PCRE regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenpcre_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [XPath regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenxpath_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [POSIX regular expression](javascript:call_link\('abenposix_regex_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (obsolete).
