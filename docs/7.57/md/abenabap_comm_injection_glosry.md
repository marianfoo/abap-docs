ABAP command injection

Injection of malicious ABAP statements by an attacker. An ABAP statement injection is possible if statements or parts of statements that are transferred from the outside and then are passed to the [runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") go unchecked.
