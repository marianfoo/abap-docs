ABAP Messaging Channels

AMC for short. Framework for communication between the [ABAP programs](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of an [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenas_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Communication is enabled by sending and receiving [data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as messages across the boundaries of an [AS instance](javascript:call_link\('abenas_instance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
