ABAP Platform Classic

[ABAP Platform](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_platform_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") variant that is delivered with [SAP S/4HANA](javascript:call_link\('abensap_s4hana_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Compared to an [ABAP Platform Strict](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_platform_strict_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), there are no restrictions regarding [ABAP language versions](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_version_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), usage of tools or access to [repository objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). ABAP development is done in a [classic ABAP development environment](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_abap_dev_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
