ABAP Platform Classic

[ABAP Platform](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_platform_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") variant that is delivered with [SAP S/4HANA](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensap_s4hana_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Compared to an [ABAP Platform Strict](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_platform_strict_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), there are no restrictions regarding [ABAP language versions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_version_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), usage of tools or access to [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). ABAP development is done in a [classic ABAP development environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclassic_abap_dev_envir_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
