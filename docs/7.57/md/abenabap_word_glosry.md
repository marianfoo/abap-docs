ABAP word

Word of the ABAP programming language. ABAP words are the [tokens](javascript:call_link\('abentoken_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP statement](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and express its semantics. In ABAP statements, ABAP words are either used as [ABAP keywords](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_keyword_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or as additions. A single ABAP word can be both a keyword and an addition. Combinations of ABAP words form [ABAP language elements](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [ABAP language element additions](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_language_elmnt_add_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
