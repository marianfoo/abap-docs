ABAP Development Tools

ADT for short. Eclipse-based tools for editing [repository objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP Repository](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_repository_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and their components. The ADT currently covers a subset of the [ABAP Workbench](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_workbench_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") functions. In the case of newer repository objects, sometimes only ADT can be used.
