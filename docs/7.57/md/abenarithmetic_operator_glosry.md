arithmetic operator

Combines two numeric [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in an [arithmetic expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarithmetic_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Arithmetic operators are [+](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\-](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [\*](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [/](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [DIV](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), [MOD](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm), and [\*\*](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenarith_operators.htm).
