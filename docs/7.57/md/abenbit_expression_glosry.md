bit expression

Formulation of a binary operation in a [calculation expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencalculation_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The result of a bit expression is a byte-like value. Bit expressions occur in [read positions of specific statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperands_expressions.htm).
