bit operator

Is applied to an [operand](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or combines two operands of a [bit expression](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The possible bit operators are [BIT-NOT](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_operators.htm'\)), [BIT-AND](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_operators.htm'\)), [BIT-OR](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_operators.htm'\)), and [BIT-XOR](javascript:call_link\('abenbit_operators.htm'\)).
