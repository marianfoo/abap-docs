BOPF-managed RAP draft provider

Draft provider of [BOs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with BOPF-managed draft. The [draft handling](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_draft_handling_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is managed by BOPF.
