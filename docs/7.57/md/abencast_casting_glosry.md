casting

Handling of a [data object](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") by assuming a specific [data type](javascript:call_link\('abendata_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Explicit casting is possible in the statement [ASSIGN](javascript:call_link\('abapassign.htm'\)) with the addition [ASSIGNING](javascript:call_link\('abapread_table_outdesc.htm'\)) when processing internal tables, for some assignments of [actual parameters](javascript:call_link\('abenactual_parameter_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to [formal parameters](javascript:call_link\('abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and as a [downcast](javascript:call_link\('abendown_cast_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [upcast](javascript:call_link\('abenup_cast_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [assignments](javascript:call_link\('abenassignment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") between [reference variables](javascript:call_link\('abenreference_variable_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). An implicit casting sometimes takes place when handling [operands](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in certain [operand positions](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
