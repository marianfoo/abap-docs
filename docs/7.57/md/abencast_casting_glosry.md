casting

Handling of a [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") by assuming a specific [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Explicit casting is possible in the statement [ASSIGN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapassign.htm) with the addition [ASSIGNING](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapread_table_outdesc.htm) when processing internal tables, for some assignments of [actual parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenactual_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to [formal parameters](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and as a [downcast](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendown_cast_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [upcast](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenup_cast_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [assignments](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenassignment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") between [reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). An implicit casting sometimes takes place when handling [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in certain [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
