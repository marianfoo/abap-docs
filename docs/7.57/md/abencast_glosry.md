cast

In ABAP, see [casting](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencast_casting_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a cast is a type conversion. In [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the operator [CAST](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_cast.htm) is used. In the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the operator CAST is used ([CDS view entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_cast_expression_v2.htm), [CDS DDIC-based views (obsolete)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_cast_expression_v1.htm)).
