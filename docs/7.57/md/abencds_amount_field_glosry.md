CDS amount field

Component of a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of data type CURR, DECFLOAT34, DEC, or FLTP in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). It is used for entering an amount in a particular currency. The content of an amount field is interpreted as an integer in the smallest unit of the currency. An ABAP CDS amount field must be linked with a [currency key](javascript:call_link\('abencurrency_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the same CDS entity that specifies the currency and the number of decimal places.
