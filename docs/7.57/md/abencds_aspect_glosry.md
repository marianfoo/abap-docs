CDS aspect

Object used for the definition of user-specific values and their use in the conditions of a [CDS role](javascript:call_link\('abencds_role_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Currently, the predefined aspects pfcg\_auth and user are available, plus the self-defined aspects aspect.
