CDS composition

Special [CDS association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that defines a parent-child relationship. The [composition source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencomposition_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is the [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [composition target](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencomposition_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is the [child entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") CDS compositions define the [CDS composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
