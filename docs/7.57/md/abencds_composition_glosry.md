CDS composition

Special [CDS association](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that defines a parent-child relationship. The [composition source](javascript:call_link\('abencomposition_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is the [parent entity](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and the [composition target](javascript:call_link\('abencomposition_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is the [child entity](javascript:call_link\('abenchild_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") CDS compositions define the [CDS composition tree](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
