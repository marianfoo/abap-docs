CDS composition tree

Tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that consists of [parent entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [child entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are themselves defined using [CDS compositions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_composition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [to-parent associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abento_parent_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The top parent entity of a CDS composition tree is the [root entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenroot_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that represents the business object.
