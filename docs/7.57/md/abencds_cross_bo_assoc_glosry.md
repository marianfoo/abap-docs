cross-BO association

[Association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not part of a [RAP business object's](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), but has an [association target](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenassociation_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is part of a foreign business object.
