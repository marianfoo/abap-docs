CDS/CTE association path

Combined sequence of [CDS associations](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [CTE associations](javascript:call_link\('abencte_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that associate [CDS entities](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [common table expressions](javascript:call_link\('abencommon_table_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with [association targets](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). CDS or CTE association paths can be specified in the form of [SQL path expressions](javascript:call_link\('abensql_path_expression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
