CDS projected entity

Used in the context of [CDS projection views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_projection_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [CDS view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is used as basis of a CDS projection view, and that is defined after the keyword AS PROJECTION ON is called projected entity.
