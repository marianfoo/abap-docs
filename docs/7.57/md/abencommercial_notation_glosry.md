commercial notation

Representation of a number as a string of digits with a possible [decimal separator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendecimal_point_separat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and with the plus or minus sign after the digits.
