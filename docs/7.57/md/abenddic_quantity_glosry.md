DDIC quantity field

Component of a [DDIC structure](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_structure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or [DDIC database table](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), or [DDIC view](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_view_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of type QUAN defined in the [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A quantity field must be linked to a [unit key](javascript:call_link\('abenunit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a structure, database table, or view. This key determines the unit of the quantity field.
