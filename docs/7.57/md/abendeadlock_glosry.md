deadlock

Blocked access to shared files, such as of a [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), due to an incorrect locking concept that prevents competing access, or an incorrect application of a locking concept.
