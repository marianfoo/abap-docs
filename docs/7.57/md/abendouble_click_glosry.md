double-click

Selecting a [screen element](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") by double-clicking with the mouse. In the [SAP GUI](javascript:call_link\('abensap_gui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), double-clicking with the mouse always has the same effect as selecting the [function key](javascript:call_link\('abenfunction_key_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") F2.
