dynpro flow logic

Procedural part of a [dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with its own programming language, similar to ABAP. Executed in [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It is separated into the [processing blocks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [PROCESS AFTER INPUT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/dynpprocess.htm), [PROCESS BEFORE OUTPUT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/dynpprocess.htm), [PROCESS ON  HELP-REQUEST](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/dynpprocess.htm), and [PROCESS ON VALUE-REQUEST](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/dynpprocess.htm), which react to the [dynpro events](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendynpro_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [PAI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpai_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [PBO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpbo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [POH](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpoh_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [POV](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpov_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and call [dialog modules](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendialog_module_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the corresponding [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
