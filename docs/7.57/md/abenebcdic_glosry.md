EBCDIC

Short for Extended Binary Coded Decimal Interchange Code. An 8-bit [character set](javascript:call_link\('abencharacter_set_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") based on [BCD](javascript:call_link\('abenbcd_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In general, EBCDIC consists of the same characters as [ASCII](javascript:call_link\('abenascii_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
