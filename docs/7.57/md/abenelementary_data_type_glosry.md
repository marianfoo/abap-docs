elementary data type

[Data type](javascript:call_link\('abendata_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of fixed or variable length that is neither [structured](javascript:call_link\('abenstructured_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), nor a [table type](javascript:call_link\('abentable_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [reference type](javascript:call_link\('abenreference_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In particular, the [built-in ABAP types](javascript:call_link\('abenbuiltin_abap_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are elementary.
