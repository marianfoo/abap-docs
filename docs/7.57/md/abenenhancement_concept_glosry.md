enhancement concept

Concept for enhancing [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") without modifying them. Implemented by the [ABAP Enhancement Framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenenhancement_framework_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")
