enhancement

Addition to a [repository object](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is managed by the [ABAP Enhancement Framework](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and changes its behavior without modification. An enhancement can be made by creating [enhancement implementation elements](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_impl_elem_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for explicitly defined or implicitly available [enhancement options](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_point_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Enhancements are switchable using the [Switch Framework](javascript:call_link\('abenswitch_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
