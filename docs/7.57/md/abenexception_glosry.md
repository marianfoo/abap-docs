exception

Error situation during execution of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Exceptions are [catchable](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencatchable_exception_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (statements [TRY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abaptry.htm), [CATCH](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapcatch_try.htm)) or [uncatchable](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenuncatchable_exception_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Uncaught exceptions result in a [runtime error](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenruntime_error_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
