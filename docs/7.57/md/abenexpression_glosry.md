expression

Part of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that has a result. An expression is made up of one or more [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), which may themselves be expressions as well, in combination with [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or special [ABAP keywords](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_word_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Expressions can be [declaration expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendeclaration_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [logical expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenlogical_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") constructed from [relational expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [constructor expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [table expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentable_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [calculation expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencalculation_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [SQL Expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [Aggregate Expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenaggregate_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [Path Expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpath_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
