field group

Line structure of an [extract dataset](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenextract_dataset_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Consists of program-global [fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenfield_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a program. Defined using the statement [FIELD-GROUPS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapfield-groups.htm).
