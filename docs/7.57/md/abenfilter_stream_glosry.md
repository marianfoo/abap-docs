filter stream

[Stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not connected directly to a [streaming data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstreaming_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [streaming data sink](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstreaming_data_sink_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") but to another stream instead. A filter stream is either a [reader stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenreader_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that reads and filters the data of another reader stream, or a [writer stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenwriter_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that writes filtered data to another writer stream.
