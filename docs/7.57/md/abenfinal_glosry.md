final

Term in [ABAP Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_objects_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A final [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") cannot have any [subclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A final [method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenmethod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") cannot be [redefined](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenredefinition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
