format setting

Part of the [language environment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenlanguage_environment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where the formatting for numbers, dates, and times is defined. Defined either by defaults in the [user master record](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenuser_master_record_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or by [country-specific formats](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencountry_formats.htm).
