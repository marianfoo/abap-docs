friend

Term in [ABAP Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_objects_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The additions [FRIENDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapclass_options.htm) and [LOCAL FRIENDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapclass_local_friends.htm) of the statement [CLASS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapclass_definition.htm) define other [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [interfaces](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as friends of the class and thus grant them access to all of their components, regardless of the [visibility section](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenvisibility_section_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or the addition [READ-ONLY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapdata_options.htm). In addition, friends of a class can create [instances](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninstance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the class without restrictions.
