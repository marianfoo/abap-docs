group loop

Loop across the groups of an [internal table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") formed by the addition [GROUP BY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abaploop_at_itab_group_by.htm) of the statement [LOOP AT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abaploop_at_itab_variants.htm). A group loop can contain [member loops](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenmember_loop_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for the evaluation of the groups.
