hierarchy association

[CDS association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [CTE association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencte_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") used to define the [parent-child relationships](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpcr_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [SQL hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [CDS hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A hierarchy association is always a [self-association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenself_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
