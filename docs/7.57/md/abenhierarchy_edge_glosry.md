hierarchy edge

Relationship between the [hierarchy nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenhierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The hierarchy edges in [SQL hierarchies](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") created by a [hierarchy generator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenhierarchy_generator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or in a [CDS hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are [parent-child relationships](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpcr_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
