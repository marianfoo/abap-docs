hierarchy

Arrangement of objects in hierarchical structures. The objects of a hierarchy are [hierarchy nodes](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are linked to each other using [hierarchy edges](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_edge_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See also [SQL hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abensql_hierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
