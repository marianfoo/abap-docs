hierarchy source

A [SQL data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that exposes a [hierarchy association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenhierarchy_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and can be used as a source of a [CDS hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or the [hierarchy generator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenhierarchy_generator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
