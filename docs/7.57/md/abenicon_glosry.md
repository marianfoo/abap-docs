icon

Graphical element of the [GUI](javascript:call_link\('abengui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for use on [pushbuttons](javascript:call_link\('abenpushbutton_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and also as a [status icon](javascript:call_link\('abenstatus_icon_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on [screens](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or in [lists](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The program SHOWICON shows a list of possible icons.
