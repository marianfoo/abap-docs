identity transformation

[XSL transformation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenxsl_transformation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") called ID delivered by SAP for reading or writing the canonical formats [asXML](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenasxml_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [asJSON](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenasjson_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Calls of this transformation are handled in special ways internally, depending on the formats involved.
