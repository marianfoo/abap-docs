index

Selected columns of a [database table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are sorted in order and stored as a copy in the [database system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and which point to the actual rows. For the database tables of an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are defined in the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), there is always a [primary index](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenprimary_index_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and additional [secondary indexes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensecondary_index_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can also be created. In [internal tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") there may be one or more [table indexes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentable_index_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
