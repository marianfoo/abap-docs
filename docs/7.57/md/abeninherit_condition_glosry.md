inheritance condition

Part of an [access condition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenaccess_condition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [CDS role](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_role_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that inherits conditions from another [CDS role](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_role_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a different [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
