internal procedure call

Call of a [processing block](javascript:call_link\('abenprocessing_block_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([procedure](javascript:call_link\('abenprocedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) of the same program. The required program does not have to be reloaded into the [internal session](javascript:call_link\('abeninternal_session_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
