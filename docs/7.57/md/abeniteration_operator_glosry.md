iteration operator

The iteration operator FOR introduces an [iteration expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeniteration_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a subexpression of a [constructor expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenconstructor_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
