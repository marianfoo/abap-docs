join condition

[SQL condition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_cond_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [CDS DDL condition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_conditional_expression_v2.htm) specified after the addition ON of a [join expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenjoin_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Also called ON condition.
