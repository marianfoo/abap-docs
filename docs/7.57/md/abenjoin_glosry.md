join

Combination of two [SQL data sources](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") into one result set, consisting of columns of both data sources. The rows of the result set are determined by the [join type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenjoin_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and by [join conditions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenjoin_condition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") between columns of the data sources. The relation between the rows of the data sources can be expressed by a [cardinality](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencardinality_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Joins can be implemented as [DDIC views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenddic_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), as [join expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenjoin_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [queries](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenquery_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or in the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Furthermore, [CDS associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [CTE associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencte_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") define join relationships that are instantiated when the associations are used in [SQL path expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensql_path_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
