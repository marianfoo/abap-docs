join type

The join type determines together with the [join conditions](javascript:call_link\('abenjoin_condition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [join](javascript:call_link\('abenjoin_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") how the rows of its result set are combined from its [SQL data sources](javascript:call_link\('abensql_data_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") support [inner joins](javascript:call_link\('abeninner_join_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [outer joins](javascript:call_link\('abenouter_join_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") ([left](javascript:call_link\('abenleft_outer_join_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [right](javascript:call_link\('abenright_outer_join_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")), and [cross joins](javascript:call_link\('abencross_join_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
