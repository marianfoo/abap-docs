LOB handle component

[Reference type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenreference_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [reference variable](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a [LOB handle structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenlob_handle_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [static type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstatic_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of such a component must be less specific or the same as the [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [LOB handle](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenlob_handle_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and must contain the [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") IF\_ABAP\_DB\_LOB\_HANDLE.
