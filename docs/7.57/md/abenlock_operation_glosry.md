RAP lock operation

[RAP BO operation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_operation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that locks [RAP BO entity instances](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_entity_inst_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") on the [persistent table](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_persistent_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") during a [RAP LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
