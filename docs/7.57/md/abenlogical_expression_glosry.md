logical expression

Formulation of a condition for [operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A logical expression is a single [relational expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrelational_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or is made up of multiple relational expressions combined by [Boolean operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenboolean_operator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The result of a logical expression is a [truth value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentruth_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), that is, either true or false. Logical expressions are used in [control statements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencontrol_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or other statements that contain conditions, as well as in [conditional expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenconditional_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [Boolean functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenboole_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
