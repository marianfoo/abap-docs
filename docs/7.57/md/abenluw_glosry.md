LUW

Short for Logical unit of work. Time between two consistent states of the [database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [SAP LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensap_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [database LUW](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
