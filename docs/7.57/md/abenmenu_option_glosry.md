menu entry

Textual [control element](javascript:call_link\('abencontrol_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [menu](javascript:call_link\('abenmenu_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is linked with a [function code](javascript:call_link\('abenfunction_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and can be selected simply by clicking it.
