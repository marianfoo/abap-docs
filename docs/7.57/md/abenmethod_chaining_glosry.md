method chaining

Chained calls of [functional methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Either a [chained method call](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenchained_method_call_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [chained attribute access](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenchained_attribute_acc_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
