namespace prefix

Prefix with five to ten characters that must contain a forward slash character (/.../) in the first and final place. The names of the [repository objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [prefix namespace](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenprefix_name_space_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are introduced by a namespace prefix.
