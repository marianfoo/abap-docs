numeric literal

[Literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that has a [numeric data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abennumeric_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and whose characters represent a number. In general ABAP, numeric literals are [untyped literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In [ABAP SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_sql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), numeric literals can also be [typed literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
