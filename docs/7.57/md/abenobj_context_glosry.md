object context

Environment of an [object](javascript:call_link\('abenobject_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Determines its visibility and lifetime. Possible contexts are [procedures](javascript:call_link\('abenprocedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [instances](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [classes](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), classes, and [programs](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
