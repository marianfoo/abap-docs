offset

Specified position for a character or a byte in a [character-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [byte-like data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenbyte_like_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") where counting begins with 0. The value of an offset can also be interpreted as the identifier for the beginning of the line, the spacing between the characters or bytes, and the end of the line.
