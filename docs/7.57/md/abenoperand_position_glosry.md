operand position

Position of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or of a [CDS data definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") at which an [operand](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is listed. There are [read](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenread_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [write positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenwrite_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In an ABAP statement, an operand position is typed with an [operand type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperand_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Usually, [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be listed at operand positions. At [expression positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenexpression_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenfunction_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be specified too.
