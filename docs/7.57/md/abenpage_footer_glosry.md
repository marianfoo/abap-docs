page footer

Reserved lines at the bottom of a page in a [list](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The page footer is defined in the [program-initiating statement](javascript:call_link\('abenprogram_init_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and can be filled during the [event](javascript:call_link\('abenevent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [END-OF-PAGE](javascript:call_link\('abapend-of-page.htm'\)).
