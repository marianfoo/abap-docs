PAI

Short for Process After Input. [Dynpro event](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_event_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") raised by a [user action](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_action_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on the [GUI](javascript:call_link\('abengui_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). At PAI time, the content of [dynpro fields](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is transferred to [data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendata_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the same name in the [ABAP program](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
