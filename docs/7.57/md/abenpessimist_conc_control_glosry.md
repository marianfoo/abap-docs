pessimistic concurrency control

Pessimistic [concurrency control](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_conc_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") prevents simultaneous modification access to data on the database by more than one user. In [RAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") this is ensured by the [RAP locking mechanism](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_locking_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
