place in a decimal number

Place in a [decimal number](javascript:call_link\('abendecimal_number_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). These can be the integer digits of a number or digits in the [fractional portion](javascript:call_link\('abendecimal_place_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In general, only those places in the fractional portion are referred to as [decimal places](javascript:call_link\('abenfract_digit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
