place in a decimal number

Place in a [decimal number](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendecimal_number_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). These can be the integer digits of a number or digits in the [fractional portion](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendecimal_place_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In general, only those places in the fractional portion are referred to as [decimal places](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenfract_digit_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
