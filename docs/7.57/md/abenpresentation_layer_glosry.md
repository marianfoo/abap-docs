presentation layer

Software layer of a [client-server system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclient_server_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). This layer analyzes [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and transfers these to the [application layer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenapplication_layer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The presentation layer of an [ABAP system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") displays its [user interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenuser_interface_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It is usually distributed across multiple [presentation servers](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpresentation_server_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
