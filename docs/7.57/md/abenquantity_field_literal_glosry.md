[Literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of type [QUAN](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenddic_builtin_types.htm) that is used to store a quantity.
