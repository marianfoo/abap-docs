quantity field

A quantity field is linked to a [unit key](javascript:call_link\('abenunit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and it is used for entering a quantity in a particular unit. Quantity fields are available in [ABAP Dictionary](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (DDIC) and in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), but the possible data types and handling differs.
