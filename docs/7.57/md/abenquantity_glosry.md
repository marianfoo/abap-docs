quantity field

A quantity field is linked to a [unit key](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenunit_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and it is used for entering a quantity in a particular unit. Quantity fields are available in [ABAP Dictionary](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_dictionary_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (DDIC) and in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), but the possible data types and handling differs.
