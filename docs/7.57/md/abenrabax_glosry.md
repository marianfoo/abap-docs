Rabax

Short for Runtime exit. Rabax is an internal SAP term describing a program crash after a [runtime error](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenruntime_error_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The corresponding [ABAP kernel](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenkernel_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") module, also called Rabax, performs the tasks required to complete the [internal session](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninternal_session_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and creates the associated [short dump](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenshort_dump_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
