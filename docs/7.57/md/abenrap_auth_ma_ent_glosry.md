RAP authorization master entity

[RAP BO entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that has its own authorization implementation. The implementation is used when requesting access to the authorization master entity or to any of its [authorization dependent entities](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_auth_dep_ent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
