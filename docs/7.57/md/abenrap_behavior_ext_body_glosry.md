RAP behavior extension body

That part of an [entity behavior extension](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_behavior_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is defined within curly brackets after the statement [extend behavior for](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenbdl_extend_beh.htm). The entity behavior extension body can define [new extension elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenbdl_new_elem_ext.htm), such as extension determinations, extension validations, and extension actions. It may also [extend existing elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenbdl_ext_elem_ext.htm), such as RAP type mapping, and determine actions.
