RAP BO draft action

Draft actions are specific [actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") whose implementation is provided by the [RAP managed draft provider](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_man_draft_provider_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). They are invoked during the lifecycle of a [draft-enabled RAP BO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendraft_rap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to manage the state of [draft](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_draft_instance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [active instances](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_active_instance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
