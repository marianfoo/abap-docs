RAP BO extension

A [repository object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that extends a [RAP BO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The following types of RAP BO extensions exist:

-   [DDIC extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenddic_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined in [DDIC DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenddic_dcl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or in [transaction](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentransaction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") SE11.
-   [CDS view extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_view_extend_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
-   [BDEF extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [CDS BDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_bdl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
-   [Service definition extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_service_def_ext_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [CDS SDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencds_sdl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
