RAP field characteristic

Property of a [RAP BO field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that defines how a field can be used on [modify access](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_modify_operation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Field characteristics can be [static](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_static_field_char_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [dynamic](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_dyn_field_char_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
