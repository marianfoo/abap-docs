internal RAP BO operation

A [RAP BO operation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_operation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can only be executed by the [RAP BO provider](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_provider_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the same [BO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
