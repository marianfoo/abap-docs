RAP managed draft provider

Part of the [RAP BO provider](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_provider_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that manages draft data on the [transactional buffer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentransactional_buffer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") during the [interaction phase](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_int_phase_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and provides [draft actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_draft_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the total ETag handling in draft-enabled [RAP BOs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
