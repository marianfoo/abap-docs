RAP total ETag

A field of a [draft-enabled RAP BO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendraft_rap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that enables [optimistic concurrency checks](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoptimistic_conc_control_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") during the transition form draft to [persistent data](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_persistent_data_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
