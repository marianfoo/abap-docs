RAP transition message

A message in the [reported](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abapeml_response.htm) structure that is independent of the state of the [entity instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_entity_inst_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Transition messages are [RAP messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_message_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that do not have a state area assigned.

There are [instance transition messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninstance_rap_trans_mes_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [static transition messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstatic_rap_trans_mes_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
