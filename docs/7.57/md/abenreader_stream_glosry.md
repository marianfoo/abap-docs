reader stream

Also referred to as [input stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninput_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Either a [data stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendata_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is connected to a [streaming data source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstreaming_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [filter stream](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenfilter_stream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that reads data from another stream. The direction of a reader stream is always away from the data source. Data can only be read from a reader stream.
