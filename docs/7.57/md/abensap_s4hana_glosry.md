SAP S/4HANA

SAP business suite that is based on the [ABAP Platform](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_platform_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and runs on the [SAP HANA](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensap_hana_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It is available in on-premise and as [SAP S/4HANA Cloud](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensap_s4hana_cloud_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in cloud deployments.
