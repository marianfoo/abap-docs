service connection

Additional [database connection](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendatabase_connection_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") from an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to the [standard database](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstandard_db_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In addition to the [standard connection](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstandard_db_connection_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), service connections to the standard database called R/3\*name can be opened for a [work process](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenwork_process_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to access the [ABAP database schema](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_db_schema_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
