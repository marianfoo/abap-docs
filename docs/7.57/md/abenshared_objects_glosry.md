shared objects

Concept of storing [objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenobject_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as [shared objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenshared_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the [shared memory](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenshared_memory_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The storage of [instances](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninstance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and of [anonymous data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenanonymous_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is supported. Any data objects can be stored as [attributes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenattribute_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of classes.
