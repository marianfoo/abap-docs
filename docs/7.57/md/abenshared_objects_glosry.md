shared objects

Concept of storing [objects](javascript:call_link\('abenobject_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as [shared objects](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [shared memory](javascript:call_link\('abenshared_memory_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The storage of [instances](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [classes](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and of [anonymous data objects](javascript:call_link\('abenanonymous_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is supported. Any data objects can be stored as [attributes](javascript:call_link\('abenattribute_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of classes.
