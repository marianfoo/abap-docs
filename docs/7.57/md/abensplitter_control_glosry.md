splitter control

Splitter controls enable the display of two [subscreens](javascript:call_link\('abensubscreen_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") next to each other or above one another. A splitter control is a [screen element](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that divides the area that it covers into two [subscreen areas](javascript:call_link\('abensubscreen_arrea_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The boundary between the areas can be moved using a [sash](javascript:call_link\('abensash_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
