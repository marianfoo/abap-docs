RAP static transition message

A [transition message](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_trans_mes_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that refers to a [RAP BO entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), but not to a specific [instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_entity_inst_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
