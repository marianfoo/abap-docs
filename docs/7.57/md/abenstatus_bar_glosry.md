status bar

Component of a [GUI window](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abengui_window_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It shows [status messages](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstatus_message_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and system information.
