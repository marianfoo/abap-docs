step loop technique

Technique in [dynpro flow logic](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_flow_logic_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for processing tabular [screen elements](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on the [screen layout](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [dynpro](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The step loop technique is used to process [table controls](javascript:call_link\('abentable_control_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and their predecessors, namely the [step loops](javascript:call_link\('abenstep_loop_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
