subroutine pool

[ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that usually contains [subroutines](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abensubroutine_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are called from other ABAP programs. It does not support its own [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
