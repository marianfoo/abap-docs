tab title

A single-line element located at the top of a [tab page](javascript:call_link\('abentab_page_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is linked to a [function code](javascript:call_link\('abenfunction_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and selects tab pages when it is clicked.
