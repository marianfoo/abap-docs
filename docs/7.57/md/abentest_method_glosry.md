test method

Special [instance method](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [test class](javascript:call_link\('abentest_class_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in which a test is implemented. Test methods are defined using the addition [FOR TESTING](javascript:call_link\('abapmethods_testing.htm'\)) of the statement [METHODS](javascript:call_link\('abapmethods.htm'\)), and called during a [test run](javascript:call_link\('abentest_run_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP Unit](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_unit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") tool.
