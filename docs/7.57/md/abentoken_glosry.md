token

Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), into which the compiler breaks down statements into tokens. Tokens are [ABAP words](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_word_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [ABAP operands](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenabap_operand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and certain special characters.
