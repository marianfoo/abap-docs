RAP transactional framework

Part of the [RAP framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_framework_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that consists of the [RAP transactional engine](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_transac_engine_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the development environment for [RAP business objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
