structural fragment view

Fragment View for short. Splits [structures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") into [alignment gaps](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenalignment_gap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [character-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [byte-like](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenbyte_like_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") areas, [numeric data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abennumeric_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [strings](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenstring_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [time stamp fields](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentimestamp_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [internal tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
