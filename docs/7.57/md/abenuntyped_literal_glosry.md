untyped literal

[Literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenliteral_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") whose [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is defined by its value or by its delimiter. Both untyped [numeric literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abennumeric_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and untyped [character literals](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abencharacter_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") exist. See also [typed literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
