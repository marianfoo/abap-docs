XSD regular expression

[Regular expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenregular_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using the syntax and semantics described under [Regular expressions in XSD 1.0 and 1.1](https://www.w3.org/XML/2008/03/xsdl-regex/re.xml). ABAP supports [XSD syntax](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenregex_xsd_syntax.htm) for regular expressions by transforming them to [PCRE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpcre_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") internally. See also [PCRE regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenpcre_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [XPath regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenxpath_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [POSIX regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_757_index_htm/7.57/en-US/abenposix_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (obsolete).
