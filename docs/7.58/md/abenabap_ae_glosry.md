ABAP Analytical Engine

The ABAP Analytical Engine implements the [OLAP](javascript:call_link\('abenolap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") concept on [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenas_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). It processes multidimensional data models to be consumed by analytical clients such as SAP Analytics Cloud. Clients communicate with the ABAP Analytical Engine using protocols such as the [InA](javascript:call_link\('abenina_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") protocol. The ABAP Analytical Engine performs calculations either on the [SAP HANA database](javascript:call_link\('abenhana_database_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or in [ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
