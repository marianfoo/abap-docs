Procedures

Procedures are processing blocks with a [parameter interface](javascript:call_link\('abenformal_parameters_oview.htm'\)) and a local [data area](javascript:call_link\('abendata_area_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that can be called from within ABAP programs. A distinction is made between:

-   [Methods](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_methods.htm'\))
-   [Function modules](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_functions.htm'\))

An obsolete type of procedure are:

-   [Subroutines](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_subroutines.htm'\))
