Procedures

Procedures are processing blocks with a [parameter interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenformal_parameters_oview.htm) and a local [data area](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendata_area_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can be called from within ABAP programs. A distinction is made between:

-   [Methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_methods.htm)
-   [Function modules](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_functions.htm)

An obsolete type of procedure are:

-   [Subroutines](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_subroutines.htm)
