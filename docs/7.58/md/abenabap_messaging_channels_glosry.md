ABAP Messaging Channels

AMC for short. Framework for communication between the [ABAP programs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [AS ABAP](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenas_abap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Communication is enabled by sending and receiving [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as messages across the boundaries of an [AS instance](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenas_instance_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
