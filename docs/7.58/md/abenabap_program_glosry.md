ABAP program

[Repository object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrepository_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that contains [ABAP source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and has a [program type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenprogram_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Additional components such as [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or the [GUI status](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abengui_status_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are assigned to an ABAP program.
