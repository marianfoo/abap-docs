RAP - Non-Standard Operations

Non-standard operations are [RAP BO operations](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_operation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that provide transactional behavior and that are user-implemented. There are two kinds of non-standard operations available:

-   [Actions](javascript:call_link\('abenbdl_action.htm'\)): user-implemented operations that change the data of a BO instance.
-   [Functions](javascript:call_link\('abenbdl_function.htm'\)): user-implemented operations that return information without any side effects.
