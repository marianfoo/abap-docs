binary floating point literal

[Numeric literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abennumeric_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that has a [binary floating point data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenbinfloat_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Available as [typed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [ABAP literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and as [typed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [untyped](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [CDS literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
