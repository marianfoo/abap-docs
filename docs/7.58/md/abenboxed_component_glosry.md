boxed component

Structured [component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencomponent_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [interface](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenoo_intf_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is not stored at the location of the definition but is [deep](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendeep_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), meaning it is managed by an internal reference. Boxed components are a mixture of [static](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstatic_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [dynamic data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendynamic_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Currently, [static boxes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstatic_box_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are supported as boxed components.
