C0 Contract Rules for DDIC Objects

The following DDIC objects can be released under the [C0 contract for extensibility](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenc0_contract_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"): [DDIC database tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddic_db_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [DDIC structures](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddic_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

The following topics list the most important C0 contract rules for DDIC objects.

-   [C0 Contract Rules for Providers of DDIC Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenc0_provider_rules_ddic.htm)
-   [C0 Contract Rules for Consumers of DDIC Objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenc0_consumer_rules_ddic.htm)
-   [Example: C0 Released DDIC Structure, Design and Consumption](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenc0_demo_ddic.htm)

The general rules for [DDIC extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddic_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are described in the topic [DDIC - Extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddic_enhancements.htm).
