CDS amount field

A component of a [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of data type CURR, DECFLOAT34, DEC, or FLTP in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). It is used for entering an amount in a particular currency. The content of an amount field is interpreted as an integer in the smallest unit of the currency. An ABAP CDS amount field must be linked with a [currency key](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencurrency_key_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the same CDS entity that specifies the currency and the number of decimal places.
