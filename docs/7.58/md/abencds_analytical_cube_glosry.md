CDS analytical cube view

[CDS view entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_v2_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [CDS DDIC-based view (obsolete)](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_v1_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") annotated with [@Analytics.dataCategory: #CUBE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_annotations_frmwrk_tables.htm).
