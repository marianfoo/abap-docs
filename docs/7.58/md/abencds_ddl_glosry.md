CDS DDL

The data definition language of [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). CDS DDL has two dialects, namely [CDS DDLA](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddla_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [CDS DDLX](javascript:call_link\('abencds_ddlx_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
