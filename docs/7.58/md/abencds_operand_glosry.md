CDS operand

Component of a [CDS data definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Defined directly or as a [formal parameter](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenformal_parameter_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [built-in function](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenbuiltin_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or [expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Operands can be combined with [operators](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenoperator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to form [expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenexpression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that in turn can be listed at certain [operand positions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenoperand_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
