CDS quantity field

A component of a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of data type QUAN, DECFLOAT16, DECFLOAT34, DEC, FLTP, INT1, INT2, or INT4 in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). It is used for entering a quantity in a particular unit. An ABAP CDS quantity field must be linked with a [unit key](javascript:call_link\('abenunit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the same CDS entity that specifies the unit of measurement and the number of decimal places.
