ABAP CDS - Syntax

The syntax of the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [CDS DCL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_dcl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in ABAP CDS comprises elements of the general [SQL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abensql_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [DCL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendcl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and also makes it possible to define annotations and CDS associations. The syntax and semantics basically match the general [CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") concept.
