checkpoint

Superordinate term for [assertions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenassertion_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [breakpoints](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenbreakpoint_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or [logpoints](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenlogpoint_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The statements [ASSERT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapassert.htm), [BREAK-POINT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapbreak-point.htm), and [LOG-POINT](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abaplog-point.htm) for defining checkpoints are not operative but equip the program for test purposes. Inactive checkpoints are ignored during program execution.
