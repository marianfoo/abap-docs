CDS child entity

Any [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [CDS composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") other than the [root entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenroot_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A child entity is defined as the [composition target](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencomposition_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [to-child association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abento_child_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in its [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and has a [to-parent association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abento_parent_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to its parent entity. A child entity has exactly one parent entity.
