comment

Explanation inserted into the [ABAP source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is ignored by the [ABAP Compiler](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_compiler_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") when the program is generated. Either a [comment line](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencomment_line_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or an [line end comment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenendline_comment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [pseudo comment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpseudo_comment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
