ABAP contract checks

ABAP contract checks summarize the following checks:

-   [RAP BO contract](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_contract_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") checks: Check for violations of [RAP BO consumer](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_consumer_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [RAP BO provider](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_provider_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") implementations.
-   [Transactional contract](javascript:call_link\('abentransactional_contract_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") checks: Are implemented in the [controlled SAP LUW](javascript:call_link\('abencontrolled_sap_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") concept. Check for transactional consistency violations in a [transactional phase](javascript:call_link\('abentr_phase_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
