convertible

Two different [data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are convertible if a [conversion rule](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenconversion_rule_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") applies to them. Two [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are convertible if their data types are convertible and their content allows the corresponding conversion. Data objects of [compatible](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencompatible_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") data types are assigned to each other without a conversion.
