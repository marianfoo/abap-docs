database lock

Physical [locks](javascript:call_link\('abenlock_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for rows in [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), imposed by the [database system](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_system_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A database lock persists until the end of the current [database LUW](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_luw_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
