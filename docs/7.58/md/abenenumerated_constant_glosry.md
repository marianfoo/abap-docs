enumerated constant

A [constant](javascript:call_link\('abenconstant_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") declared in the definition of an [enumerated type](javascript:call_link\('abenenum_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that contains an [enumerated value](javascript:call_link\('abenenumerated_value_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the enumerated type.

In ABAP, an enumerated constant is a special [enumerated object](javascript:call_link\('abenenumerated_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). See also [enumerated structure](javascript:call_link\('abenenumerated_structure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

In ABAP CDS, an enumerated constant is a special [enumerated element](javascript:call_link\('abencds_enum_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
