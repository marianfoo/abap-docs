enumerated constant

A [constant](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenconstant_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") declared in the definition of an [enumerated type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenenum_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that contains an [enumerated value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenenumerated_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the enumerated type.

In ABAP, an enumerated constant is a special [enumerated object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenenumerated_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). See also [enumerated structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenenumerated_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

In ABAP CDS, an enumerated constant is a special [enumerated element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_enum_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
