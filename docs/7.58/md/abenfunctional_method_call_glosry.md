functional method call

Call of a [functional method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenfunctional_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in a suitable [read position](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenread_position_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), where the [return value](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenreturn_value_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the method is used as an [ABAP operand](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_operand_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
