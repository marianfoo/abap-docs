GROUP BY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abaploop_at_itab_group_by.htm) or [FOR GROUPS ... OF](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenfor_groups_of.htm).
