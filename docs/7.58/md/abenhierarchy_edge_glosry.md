hierarchy edge

Relationship between the [hierarchy nodes](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The hierarchy edges in [SQL hierarchies](javascript:call_link\('abensql_hierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") created by a [hierarchy generator](javascript:call_link\('abenhierarchy_generator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or in a [CDS hierarchy](javascript:call_link\('abencds_hierarchy_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are [parent-child relationships](javascript:call_link\('abenpcr_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
