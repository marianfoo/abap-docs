hierarchy

Arrangement of objects in hierarchical structures. The objects of a hierarchy are [hierarchy nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are linked to each other using [hierarchy edges](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenhierarchy_edge_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
