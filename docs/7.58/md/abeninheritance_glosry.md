inheritance

Generalization-specialization relationship between [classes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Inheritance means that new [subclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abensubclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are derived by adopting all [class components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenclass_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") from [superclasses](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abensuperclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A subclass can also be made more specific by declaring new components and [redefining](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenredefinition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [methods](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenmethod_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
