integer literal

[Numeric literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abennumeric_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [integer type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abeninteger_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Available in ABAP ([ABAP literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) and in CDS ([CDS literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")). Available as [typed literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and as [untyped literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
