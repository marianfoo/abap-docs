integer literal

[Numeric literal](javascript:call_link\('abennumeric_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of an [integer type](javascript:call_link\('abeninteger_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Available in ABAP ([ABAP literal](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) and in CDS ([CDS literal](javascript:call_link\('abencds_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")). Available as [typed literal](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and as [untyped literal](javascript:call_link\('abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
