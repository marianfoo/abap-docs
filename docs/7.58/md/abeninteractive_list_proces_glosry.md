interactive list processing

[List processing](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_processing_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that takes place after [user actions](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_action_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") on [lists](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The associated [event keywords](javascript:call_link\('abenevent_keyword_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are [AT LINE-SELECTION](javascript:call_link\('abapat_line-selection.htm'\)), [AT USER-COMMAND](javascript:call_link\('abapat_user-command.htm'\)), and [AT PFnn](javascript:call_link\('abapat_pfnn.htm'\)).
