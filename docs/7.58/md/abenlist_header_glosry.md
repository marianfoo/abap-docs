list header

Header of a list. Composed of the list title in the [title bar](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentitle_bar_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and of possible column headers. These can be maintained as part of the [text elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentext_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") for the [standard page header](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstandard_page_header_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
