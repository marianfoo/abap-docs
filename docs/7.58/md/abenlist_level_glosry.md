list level

Position of a [list](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in the [list buffer](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_buffer_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A [basic list](javascript:call_link\('abenbasic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") has the [list index](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_index_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") 0; the list index of a [details list](javascript:call_link\('abendetails_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") is greater than 0. Content of the [system field](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [sy-lsind](javascript:call_link\('abensystem_fields.htm'\)).
