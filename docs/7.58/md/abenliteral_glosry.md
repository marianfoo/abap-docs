literal

Direct character-like specification of a value in the [ABAP source code](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP program](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or in a [CDS object](javascript:call_link\('abencds_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). A distinction is drawn between [typed literals](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [untyped literals](javascript:call_link\('abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
