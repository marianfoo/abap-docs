literal operator

The literal operator & joins two [text field literals](javascript:call_link\('abentext_field_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or two [text string literals](javascript:call_link\('abentext_string_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as a single literal or two [string templates](javascript:call_link\('abenstring_template_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") as a single string template. The literal operator is supported in [ABAP literals](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), but not in [CDS literals](javascript:call_link\('abencds_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
