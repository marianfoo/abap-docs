macro

Combination of a statement sequence for internal reuse of [source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abensource_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") within a program. In [ABAP source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), macros are defined between [DEFINE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapdefine.htm) and [END-OF-DEFINITION](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapend-of-definition.htm) or in database table TRMAC.
