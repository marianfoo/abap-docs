managed RAP BO provider

Part of the [RAP BO provider](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrap_bo_provider_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that provides [managed transactional buffers](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenmanaged_trans_buff_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and managed behavior of [managed RAP BOs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenmanaged_rap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") based on their [RAP behavior definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_behavior_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
