Selection Screens in ABAP Release 6.40

Modification

Arrangement of Radio Buttons

From ABAP release 6.40, the addition [RADIOBUTTON](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapparameters_screen.htm) of the statement [PARAMETERS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abapparameters_screen.htm) displays defined radio buttons in the first position of the selection screen and the associated text on the right. Before ABAP release 6.40, radio buttons were displayed on the right of the text.
