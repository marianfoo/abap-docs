numeric text literal

[Literal](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that contains [numeric text](javascript:call_link\('abennumeric_text_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and has the length of the specified characters. Available as [typed](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [ABAP literal](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and as [typed](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [untyped](javascript:call_link\('abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [CDS literal](javascript:call_link\('abencds_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
