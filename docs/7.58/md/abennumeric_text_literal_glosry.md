numeric text literal

[Literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that contains [numeric text](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abennumeric_text_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and has the length of the specified characters. Available as [typed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [ABAP literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and as [typed](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [untyped](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [CDS literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
