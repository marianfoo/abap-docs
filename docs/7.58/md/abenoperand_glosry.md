operand

Component of an [ABAP statement](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or of a [CDS data definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Operands can be combined with [operators](javascript:call_link\('abenoperator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to form [expressions](javascript:call_link\('abenexpression_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that in turn can be listed at certain [operand positions](javascript:call_link\('abenoperand_position_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

There are different types of operands:

-   [ABAP operands](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_operand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")
-   [CDS operands](javascript:call_link\('abencds_operand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")
