POSIX regular expression

[Regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenregular_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in accordance with [POSIX](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/POSIX) standard 1003.2. ABAP supports [POSIX syntax](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenregex_posix_syntax.htm) for regular expressions by implementing the [Boost.Regex Library](http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_31_0/libs/regex/doc/index.html) in the [ABAP kernel](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenkernel_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Regular expressions in POSIX syntax are obsolete in ABAP and should be migrated to [PCRE regular expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpcre_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [XPath regular expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenxpath_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [XSD regular expressions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenxsd_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
