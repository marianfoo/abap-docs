RAP BO service projection entity

A component of a RAP BO service projection. It describes the [RAP data model](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrap_data_model_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is included in the service projection. The RAP BO service projection entity is represented by a [CDS projection view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_projection_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the provider contract [TRANSACTIONAL\_QUERY](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_pv_provider_contract.htm).
