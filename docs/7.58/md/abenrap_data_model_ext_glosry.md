RAP data model extension

Extension of the data model of a [RAP BO](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). May consist of [DDIC extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenddic_extension_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [CDS entity extensions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_entity_extend_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
