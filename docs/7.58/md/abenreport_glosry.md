report

The process of reading and displaying data, for example in a [list](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Outdated term for an [executable program](javascript:call_link\('abenexecutable_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that only executes [reporting](javascript:call_link\('abenreporting_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
