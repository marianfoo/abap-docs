RESTful

Programming models and their services, APIs, and protocols are called RESTful if they meet the [REST](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrest_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") principles. See also [ABAP RESTful Application Programming Model](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenarap_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
