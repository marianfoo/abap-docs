SADL

Short for Service Adaptation Definition Language. Infrastructure for model-based reading and processing of data. The [RAP runtime framework](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_runt_framework_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") uses SADL internally for its [query engine](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_query_engine_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and its orchestration framework, which controls the runtime behavior of [RAP business objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
