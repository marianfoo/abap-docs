self-association

[Association](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in which the [association source](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and the [association target](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are the same. Also referred to as reflexive associations.
