Standard ABAP

[Unrestricted ABAP language version](javascript:call_link\('abenunrestricted_version_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with unrestricted language scope and unrestricted access to [repository objects](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). For Standard ABAP, the [Unicode checks](javascript:call_link\('abenunicode_check_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are effective that are required in an [Unicode systems](javascript:call_link\('abenunicode_system_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Standard ABAP is the [ABAP language version](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_version_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") for [classic ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
