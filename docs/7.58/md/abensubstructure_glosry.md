substructure

Structured [component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstructure_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [nested structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abennested_structure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The direct predecessor of a substructure is its [superstructure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abensuperstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), A substructure can be declared as a [boxed component](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenboxed_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
