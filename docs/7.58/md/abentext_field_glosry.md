text field

[Character-like data object](javascript:call_link\('abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of ABAP or elementary object of the database with a [text field type](javascript:call_link\('abentext_field_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

In [dynpros](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), a text field is a [screen element](javascript:call_link\('abenscreen_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is used purely for displaying text and on which no [user actions](javascript:call_link\('abenuser_action_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are possible. A text field of a dynpro is not linked to a [dynpro field](javascript:call_link\('abendynpro_field_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
