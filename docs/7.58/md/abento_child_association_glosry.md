CDS to-child association

A specialized [CDS association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenabap_core_data_services_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is defined in the [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and points to the [child entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using the syntax statement [COMPOSITION OF](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_composition_v2.htm). In combination with the [to-parent association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abento_parent_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), they define a [CDS composition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_composition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") relationship. The CDS compositions are the basis for the [CDS composition tree](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenrap_bo_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
