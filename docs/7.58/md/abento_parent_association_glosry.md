CDS to-parent association

A specialized [CDS association](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_core_data_services_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is defined in the [child entity](javascript:call_link\('abenchild_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and points to the [parent entity](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") using the syntax statement [ASSOCIATION TO PARENT](javascript:call_link\('abencds_to_parent_assoc_v2.htm'\)). In combination with the [to-child association](javascript:call_link\('abento_child_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), they define a [CDS composition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") relationship. The CDS compositions are the basis for the [CDS composition tree](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_tree_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
