upcast

Also referred to as a widening cast. [Assignment](javascript:call_link\('abenassignment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") between [reference variables](javascript:call_link\('abenreference_variable_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), where the [static type](javascript:call_link\('abenstatic_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the target variables is more general or identical to the static type of the source variables. An upcast does not have to be performed explicitly. See [downcast](javascript:call_link\('abendown_cast_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
