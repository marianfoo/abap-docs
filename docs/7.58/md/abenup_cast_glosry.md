upcast

Also referred to as a widening cast. [Assignment](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenassignment_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") between [reference variables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenreference_variable_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), where the [static type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenstatic_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the target variables is more general or identical to the static type of the source variables. An upcast does not have to be performed explicitly. See [downcast](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendown_cast_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
