visibility section

The [declaration part](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abendeclaration_part_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") can be divided into up to three visibility sections. These areas are [public](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpublic_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [protected](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenprotected_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), or [private](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenprivate_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). They define the visibility of the [components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenclass_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the class and therefore the [interfaces](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenintf_gen_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the class towards all users allowed by the [package check](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpackage_concept_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") concept.
