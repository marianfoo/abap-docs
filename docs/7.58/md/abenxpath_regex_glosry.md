XPath regular expression

[Regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenregular_expression_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") using the syntax and semantics described under [Query 1.0 and XPath 2.0 Functions and Operators](https://www.w3.org/TR/xquery-operators/#regex-syntax). ABAP supports [XPath syntax](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenregex_xpath_syntax.htm) for regular expressions by transforming them to [PCRE](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpcre_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") internally. See also [PCRE regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenpcre_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [XSD regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenxsd_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [POSIX regular expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_758_index_htm/7.58/en-US/abenposix_regex_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") (obsolete).
