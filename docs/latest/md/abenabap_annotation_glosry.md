ABAP annotation

[SAP annotation](javascript:call_link\('abensap_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that can be specified in [CDS source code](javascript:call_link\('abencds_source_code_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and that is evaluated by the ABAP Core Data Services framework, namely the [ABAP runtime framework](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") itself. Any other [CDS annotations](javascript:call_link\('abencds_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are [framework-specific annotations](javascript:call_link\('abenfrmwrk_annotation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and must be evaluated by their own frameworks.
