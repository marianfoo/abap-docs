ABAP word

Word of the ABAP programming language. ABAP words are the [tokens](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentoken_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [ABAP statement](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_statement_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and express its semantics. In ABAP statements, ABAP words are either used as [ABAP keywords](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_keyword_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or as additions. A single ABAP word can be both a keyword and an addition. Combinations of ABAP words form [ABAP language elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_language_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or [ABAP language element additions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_language_elmnt_add_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
