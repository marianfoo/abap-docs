alignment gap

Bytes within a [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are inserted either before [structure components](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_component_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with alignment requirements or before the end of the structure to provide the necessary [alignment](javascript:call_link\('abenalignment_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Alignment gaps contribute to the length of a structure. The binary content of alignment gaps is not defined and must not be evaluated.
