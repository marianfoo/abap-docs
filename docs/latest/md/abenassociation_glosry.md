association

Relationship between [association sources](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenassociation_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and [association targets](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenassociation_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") defined by conditions.

The following types of associations are available:

-   [CDS associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")
-   [CTE associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencte_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")
-   [mesh associations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenmesh_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
