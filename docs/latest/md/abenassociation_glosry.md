association

Relationship between [association sources](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_source_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [association targets](javascript:call_link\('abenassociation_target_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") defined by conditions.

The following types of associations are available:

-   [CDS associations](javascript:call_link\('abencds_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")
-   [CTE associations](javascript:call_link\('abencte_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")
-   [mesh associations](javascript:call_link\('abenmesh_association_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
