boxed component

Structured [component](javascript:call_link\('abencomponent_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of a [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [class](javascript:call_link\('abenclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or [interface](javascript:call_link\('abenoo_intf_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is not stored at the location of the definition but is [deep](javascript:call_link\('abendeep_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), meaning it is managed by an internal reference. Boxed components are a mixture of [static](javascript:call_link\('abenstatic_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [dynamic data objects](javascript:call_link\('abendynamic_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Currently, [static boxes](javascript:call_link\('abenstatic_box_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are supported as boxed components.
