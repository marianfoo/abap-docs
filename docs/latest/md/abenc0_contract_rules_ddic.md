The following topics list the most important C0 contract rules for DDIC objects.

-   [C0 Contract Rules for Consumers of DDIC Objects](javascript:call_link\('abenc0_consumer_rules_ddic.htm'\))
-   [Example: C0 Released DDIC Structure, Design and Consumption](javascript:call_link\('abenc0_demo_ddic.htm'\))

The general rules for [DDIC extensions](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_extension_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are described in the topic [DDIC - Extensions](javascript:call_link\('abenddic_enhancements.htm'\)).
