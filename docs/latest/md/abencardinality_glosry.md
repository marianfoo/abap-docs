cardinality

In a [join](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenjoin_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of two [SQL data sources](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abensql_data_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), the cardinality expresses the relation between the rows of the data sources. Typical cardinalities are one-to-one (1:1), one-to-many (1:n), many-to-one (n:1), and many-to-many (n:m).
