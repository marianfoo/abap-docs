CDS DCL aspect

Object used for the definition of user-specific values and their use in the conditions of a [CDS role](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_role_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Currently, the predefined aspects pfcg\_auth and user are available, plus the user-defined aspects aspect.
