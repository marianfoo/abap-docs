CDS composition

A CDS composition consists of a [to-parent association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abento_parent_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and a [to-child association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abento_child_association_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A CDS composition defines an existential dependency between two entities, where the [child entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenchild_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") cannot exist without the [parent entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenparent_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
