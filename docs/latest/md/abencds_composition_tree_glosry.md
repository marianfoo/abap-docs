CDS composition tree

A tree-like hierarchical structure of a [RAP business object](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that consists of [parent entities](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and [child entities](javascript:call_link\('abenparent_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are themselves defined using [CDS compositions](javascript:call_link\('abencds_composition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The top parent entity of a CDS composition tree is the [root entity](javascript:call_link\('abenroot_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") which represents the business object.
