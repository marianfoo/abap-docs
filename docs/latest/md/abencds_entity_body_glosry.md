entity behavior body

That part of an [entity behavior definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_entity_bdef_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is defined within curly brackets. The entity behavior body can include [RAP BO operations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenrap_bo_operation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [validations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenrap_validation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), [determinations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenrap_determination_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and other elements.
