entity behavior body

That part of an [entity behavior definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_bdef_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is defined within curly brackets. The entity behavior body can include [RAP BO operations](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_operation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [validations](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_validation_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [determinations](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_determination_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and other elements.
