CDS source field

A field of a data source of a [CDS entity](javascript:call_link\('abencds_entity_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that can be used as a [CDS element](javascript:call_link\('abencds_element_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and at different operand positions of that CDS entity.
