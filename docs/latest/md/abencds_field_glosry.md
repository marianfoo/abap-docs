CDS source field

A field of a data source of a [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that can be used as a [CDS element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and at different operand positions of that CDS entity.
