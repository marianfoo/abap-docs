CDS metadata extension

Short MDE. A [CDS object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the [ABAP CDS](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_cds_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is defined in [DDLX source code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenddlx_source_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with [language elements](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_f1_ddlx_syntax.htm) of the [CDS DDL](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_ddl_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). In a CDS metadata extension, [CDS annotations](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are specified for a [CDS entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_entity_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") outside its [data definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_data_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). A CDS metadata extension is always assigned to a layer such as industry, partner or customer.
