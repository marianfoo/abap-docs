CDS projected entity

Used in the context of [CDS projection views](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_projection_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). The [CDS view entity](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_v2_view_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is used as the basis of a CDS projection view, and that is defined after the addition AS PROJECTION ON is called projected entity.
