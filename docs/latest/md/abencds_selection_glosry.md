analytical selection

Also called restricted measure. An analytical selection is an element of a [CDS analytical projection view](javascript:call_link\('abencds_analytical_pv_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that is newly added in the projection layer. A selection is defined by means of a [selection-related case expression](javascript:call_link\('abencds_case_expression_apv.htm'\)).
