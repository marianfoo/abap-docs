analytical selection

Also called restricted measure. An analytical selection is an element of a [CDS analytical projection view](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_analytical_pv_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is newly added in the projection layer. A selection is defined by means of a [selection-related case expression](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_case_expression_apv.htm).
