classic list

List for short. Medium used for the structured and formatted output of data. List output is either written to a [list buffer](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenlist_buffer_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a [screen list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenscreen_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and displayed on a [list dynpro](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenlist_dynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") or is sent to the [SAP spool system](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abensap_spool_system_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") as a [spool list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenspool_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
