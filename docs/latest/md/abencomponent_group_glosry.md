component group

Also [named include](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abennamed_include_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Group of [structure components](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenstructure_component_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that are included from another [structure](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenstructure_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") under one name. The components can be addressed by this name.
