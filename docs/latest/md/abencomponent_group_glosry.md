component group

Also [named include](javascript:call_link\('abennamed_include_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Group of [structure components](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_component_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that are included from another [structure](javascript:call_link\('abenstructure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") under one name. The components can be addressed by this name.
