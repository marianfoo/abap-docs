constant

In ABAP, a [named data object](javascript:call_link\('abennamed_data_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") whose value cannot be changed during the runtime of an [ABAP program](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_program_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") or a [procedure](javascript:call_link\('abenprocedure_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), constants appear as components of [CDS enumerated types](javascript:call_link\('abencds_enum_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
