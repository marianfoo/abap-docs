DML

Short for Data Manipulation Language. Subset of [SQL](javascript:call_link\('abensql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). The statements in DML perform [CRUD](javascript:call_link\('abencrud_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") operations on [relational](javascript:call_link\('abenrelational_database_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") [database tables](javascript:call_link\('abendatabase_table_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In ABAP, DML is mapped by [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
