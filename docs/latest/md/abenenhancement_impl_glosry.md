enhancement implementation

A [repository object](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") used for the administration of [enhancements](javascript:call_link\('abenenhancement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of other repository objects. Enhancement implementations are components of a hierarchical tree structure of which the branches represent [composite](javascript:call_link\('abencomp_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") enhancement implementations, and the leaves are [simple enhancement implementations](javascript:call_link\('abensimple_enhancement_impl_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
