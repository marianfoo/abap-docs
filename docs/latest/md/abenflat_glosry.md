flat

Property of a [data type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendata_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), where the content of its [data objects](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") represents the actual work data. All [elementary data types](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenelementary_data_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") except [string](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenbuiltin_types_character.htm) and [xstring](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenbuiltin_types_byte.htm) are flat. See also [deep](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendeep_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
