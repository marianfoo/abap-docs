hierarchy aggregate navigator

[Hierarchy navigator](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenhierarchy_navigator_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that accesses specific [hierarchy nodes](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenhierarchy_node_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of an [SQL hierarchy](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abensql_hierarchy_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and applies [aggregate functions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenaggregate_function_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") to them.
