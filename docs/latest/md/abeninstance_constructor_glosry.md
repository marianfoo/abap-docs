instance constructor

[Constructor](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenconstructor_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") declared under the name [constructor](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenconstructor.htm) as an [instance method](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abeninstance_method_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of a [class](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenclass_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and which is automatically called when the class is [instantiated](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abeninstantiation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and after the [object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenobject_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is created.
