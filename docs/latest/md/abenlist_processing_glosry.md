list processing

Processing of a [screen list](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenscreen_list_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") encapsulated in the [ABAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [dynpro event](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendynpro_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") [PAI](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenpai_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") is passed to the [ABAP program](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_program_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in the form of [list events](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenlist_event_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
