numeric literal

[Literal](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that has a [numeric data type](javascript:call_link\('abennumeric_data_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and whose characters represent a number.

-   In general ABAP, numeric literals are [untyped literals](javascript:call_link\('abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
-   In [ABAP SQL](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_sql_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), numeric literals can also be [typed literals](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
-   In [ABAP CDS](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_cds_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), numeric literals can also be [typed literals](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
