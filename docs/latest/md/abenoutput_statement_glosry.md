output statement

Statement for writing into a [list](javascript:call_link\('abenclassic_list_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). Output statements are [WRITE](javascript:call_link\('abapwrite-.htm'\)) and [ULINE](javascript:call_link\('abapuline.htm'\)). There are also statements for positioning the [list cursor](javascript:call_link\('abenlist_cursor_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
