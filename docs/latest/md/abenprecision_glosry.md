precision

Number of significant [places](javascript:call_link\('abenfract_digit_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [decimal floating point number](javascript:call_link\('abendecfloat_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). These are the places of the mantissa after the leading zeros. The precision is at least one and at most the length of the mantissa (16 or 34). See also [scaling](javascript:call_link\('abenscale_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
