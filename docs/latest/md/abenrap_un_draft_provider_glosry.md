RAP unmanaged draft provider

Draft [provider](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_provider_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of [BOs](javascript:call_link\('abenrap_bo_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with unmanaged draft.
