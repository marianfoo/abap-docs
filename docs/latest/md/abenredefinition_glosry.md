redefinition

Term in [ABAP Objects](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_objects_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"). In a redefinition, an [instance method](javascript:call_link\('abeninstance_method_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in a [subclass](javascript:call_link\('abensubclass_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") can be reimplemented without changing the [parameter interface](javascript:call_link\('abenparameter_interface_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
