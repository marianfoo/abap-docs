Restricted [ABAP development environment](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_dev_envir_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") (aka [embedded Steampunk](javascript:call_link\('abenembedded_steampunk_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) for customer developments in [ABAP Platform Cloud](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_platform_cloud_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
