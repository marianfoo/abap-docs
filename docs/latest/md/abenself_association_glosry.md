self-association

[Association](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenassociation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in which the [association source](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenassociation_source_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and the [association target](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenassociation_target_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are the same. Also referred to as reflexive associations.
