service binding

SB for short. A service binding is a [repository object](javascript:call_link\('abenrepository_object_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") that binds a [CDS service definition](javascript:call_link\('abencds_service_definition_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") to a [RESTful](javascript:call_link\('abenrestful_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") protocol such as [OData](javascript:call_link\('abenodata_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and publishes it as a [business service](javascript:call_link\('abenbusiness_service_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") in [AS ABAP](javascript:call_link\('abenas_abap_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
