streaming

The sequential and segmental transfer of data or the transfer of [streams](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenstream_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"). Streaming is intended for all possible resources and is currently implemented for [LOBs](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenlob_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") in ABAP SQL, [strings](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenstring_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), and [internal tables](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abeninternal_table_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
