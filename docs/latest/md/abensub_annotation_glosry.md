sub-annotation

[CDS annotation](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_annotation_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is specified in curly brackets [{ }](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_f1_define_annotation.htm) in an [annotation definition](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_anno_definition_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") after another annotation. When specifying a sub-annotation in [annotation syntax](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_annotation_syntax_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), curly brackets [{ }](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_annotations_syntax.htm) or [structured names](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_annotations_syntax_name.htm) separated by periods are also used.
