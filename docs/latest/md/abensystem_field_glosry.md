system field

A built-in [data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendata_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is filled depending on the context by the [ABAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and analyzed in the program.
