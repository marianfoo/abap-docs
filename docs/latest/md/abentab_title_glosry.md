tab title

A single-line element located at the top of a [tab page](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentab_page_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is linked to a [function code](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenfunction_code_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and selects tab pages when it is clicked.
