text field

[Character-like data object](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencharlike_data_object_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of ABAP or elementary object of the database with a [text field type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentext_field_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

In [dynpros](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendynpro_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry"), a text field is a [screen element](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenscreen_element_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") that is used purely for displaying text and on which no [user actions](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenuser_action_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are possible. A text field of a dynpro is not linked to a [dynpro field](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abendynpro_field_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
