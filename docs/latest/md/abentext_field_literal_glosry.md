text field literal

[Character literal](javascript:call_link\('abencharacter_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") of the type [text field type](javascript:call_link\('abentext_field_type_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") with the length of the specified characters. A text field literal can be linked to a [text symbol](javascript:call_link\('abentext_symbol_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").

Available in ABAP ([ABAP literal](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")) and in CDS ([CDS literal](javascript:call_link\('abencds_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry")). Available as [typed literal](javascript:call_link\('abentyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and as [untyped literal](javascript:call_link\('abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
