text field literal

[Character literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencharacter_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") of the type [text field type](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentext_field_type_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") with the length of the specified characters. A text field literal can be linked to a [text symbol](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentext_symbol_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").

Available in ABAP ([ABAP literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")) and in CDS ([CDS literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abencds_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry")). Available as [typed literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and as [untyped literal](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenuntyped_literal_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
