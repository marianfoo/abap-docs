token

Elementary component of an [ABAP statement](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_statement_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), into which the compiler breaks down statements into tokens. Tokens are [ABAP words](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_word_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [ABAP operands](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_operand_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), [operators](javascript:call_link\('abenoperator_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry"), and certain special characters.
