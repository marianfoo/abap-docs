UTC time stamp

[Time stamp](javascript:call_link\('abentime_stamp_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") referring to the [UTC](javascript:call_link\('abenutc_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") reference time. The time stamps of the [ABAP runtime framework](javascript:call_link\('abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") are UTC time stamps, but they are [POSIX time stamps](javascript:call_link\('abenposix_timestamp_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry") and therefore do not support [leap seconds](javascript:call_link\('abenleap_second_glosry.htm'\) "Glossary Entry").
