UTC time stamp

[Time stamp](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abentime_stamp_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") referring to the [UTC](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenutc_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") reference time. The time stamps of the [ABAP runtime framework](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenabap_runtime_frmwk_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") are UTC time stamps, but they are [POSIX time stamps](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenposix_timestamp_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry") and therefore do not support [leap seconds](https://help.sap.com/doc/abapdocu_latest_index_htm/latest/en-US/abenleap_second_glosry.htm "Glossary Entry").
